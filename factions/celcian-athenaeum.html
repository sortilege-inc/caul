Celcian Athenaeum

The Celcian Athenaeum is a scholarly faction focused on the preservation and study of magical knowledge, particularly in the arcane arts. It maintains rigorous standards and practices, training initiates in various forms of magic in a location far to the west. The faction emphasizes the responsible use of magic in alignment with the teachings of powerful mentors.

Notable members include Tamber, a former initiate expelled for performing destructive, essence-draining rituals that violated the Athenaeum's principles. The faction disapproves of such practices as they contradict its tenets of archivism. The Athenaeum has faced challenges from individuals like Ygva, another expelled witch, who engaged in dangerous magic similar to Tamber's. Her expulsion stemmed from rituals that drained magical essence, influencing the faction's stance on the responsible use of magic.

More recently, Celcian Athenaeum has emerged as a faction associated with the Celcian Athenaeum, operating within a center of higher learning that features a Sacred Pyre and magical wards. The faction is led by Dean Therion Calanthe, an elf responsible for overseeing activities and teachings. Members, including Dagga Brunnvar, an antiquarian dwarf, collect artifacts for the treasury and maintain the compound's operations. Celcian Athenaeum focuses on the concept of Ascension, with certain individuals identified as elect candidates for a ritual to ascend to a higher plane.

Upon the arrival of a party of travelers, Dagga greeted them and requested appropriate artifacts for the treasury, indicating that the dean would not be impressed without such offerings. Heyou presented his wooden left leg as a contribution, which Dagga accepted, noting its potential interest to the Keeper of Splendor. The party was shown to their quarters, where they observed the compound's magical properties and wards. During their exploration, the party encountered Dean Therion, who questioned their intentions regarding a witch named Ygva. Sylvie explained their quest for a sacred branch to reignite their township's Sacred Pyre. Inola, a member of Celcian Athenaeum, introduced the idea of Ascension and identified Heyou and Sylvie as new candidates for this process, while clarifying that others present were not among The Elect. Tensions escalated when Dean Therion attacked the party, leading to a confrontation involving multiple copies of himself and various magical effects.
