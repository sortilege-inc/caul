Faction

Goblin Engineers

The Goblin Engineers are a faction of goblins recognized for their mechanical expertise and engineering skills. They operate within an Underground Complex, specializing in constructing defenses and contraptions, including an exploding bridge. Members are typically equipped with tool belts and socket wrenches, focusing on engineering over traditional combat.

The Goblin Engineers have a pragmatic attitude toward workplace hazards, accepting injuries as a normal part of their trade. They have established a positive rapport with Heyou, whom they respect for his mechanical aptitude. Known for their dangerous approach to survival, they utilize explosives to secure their territory, rigging a bridge over an underground chasm with three-part Explosive Barrels as a defensive measure. Their confidence in handling explosives is evident, as they explained that these barrels could be shaken and thrown before detonation.

Recently, the Goblin Engineers orchestrated a strategic ambush against a group of adventurers. They provided information about a Guardian beast tethered to a Glowing Tree, which the adventurers aimed to approach for Blessed Branch. A Goblin Envoy proposed an alliance, where the adventurers would eliminate the Guardian in exchange for safe passage to take what they needed. The Guardian's territory was marked by a ring of corpses, indicating its protective role.

As the adventurers prepared for their confrontation, the Goblin Engineers showed a willingness to support them by supplying explosive powder as a gesture of goodwill. However, their leader's grim understanding of tactics became evident when previous attempts to use explosives against the creature resulted in no returns. The goblins returned to the area with additional TNT barrels and began setting more explosives, raising concerns about the safety of the Sacred Tree. Their leader dismissed the tree's sanctity, reflecting their pragmatic viewpoint.

Tensions escalated when a catastrophic accident involving a goblin carrying an explosive barrel resulted in a massive explosion that destroyed the Sacred Tree and several goblins, effectively ending any hopes of alliance. The faction's culture, focused on engineering and risk acceptance, underscores their unique survival strategy in a challenging environment. The Goblin Engineers remain a dangerous force, leveraging risk to achieve their objectives, and their recent actions have solidified their reputation as a faction willing to embrace chaos in pursuit of their goals.
