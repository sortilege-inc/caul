Faction

Aetherweavers

The Aetherweavers are a faction that harnesses magic from the Aether, contrasting with witches who draw from the Umbra. This distinction subjects them to stigma, particularly as the Umbra is associated with significant power.

They operate within Ulmira's Step, a settlement built around the remnants of a colossal titan, where giants, dwarves, and humans coexist. The town is marked by its Sacred Pyre and is largely self-sufficient, rarely seeing outsiders.

The Aetherweavers navigate local customs, which include a cautious attitude towards strangers and the presence of the Syndicate, suggesting their influence in the community. They sought guidance to rekindle a darkened pyre in Drosven’s Gate and learned that a Blessed Branch, essential for their task, is kept by Ygva, a witch associated with the Umbra, whom the townspeople generally avoid. Sarru, the dwarven pyre-keeper, confirmed the branch's location and warned of danger from seeking it, specifically the risks associated with the Umbra.

The Aetherweavers' efforts to acquire the Blessed Branch highlight the challenges they face in bridging cultural divides and the tensions surrounding Umbra practitioners.
