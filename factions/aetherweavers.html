Aetherweavers

The Aetherweavers are mages who draw their power from the vestiges of the Veiled Gods — the residual light of the departed — which sets them apart from witches, who draw instead on the Umbra. The distinction carries a stigma: some communities distrust aether-users, and the Umbra is feared for the greater power it is thought to hold.
