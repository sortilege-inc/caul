Stone Weavers

The Stone Weavers are a faction known for their craftsmanship in manipulating and shaping stone, with a strong connection to the earth. They have a historical presence in the Stoneheart Peaks, where remnants of their work, including a cleft used for shelter, can still be found.

Sylvie, linked to the Stone Weavers through personal history, is motivated to restore the mountain and its magical properties. Her dreams have revealed connections to the Stone Weavers and visions of Ygva, a figure associated with them. Sylvie's efforts to channel their energy culminated in a significant magical event, where a colossal stone body descended from the sky, showcasing the potential for the Stone Weavers' influence to return.
