Faction

Dimmers

The Dimmers are a formidable faction deeply entrenched in the criminal underworld, recognized for their operations in the shadows and their complex ties to various illicit activities. Their reputation instills caution among the local populace, particularly due to past incidents that have marred their image, such as the grim fate of two members who fell from a high bridge, serving as a stark reminder of the dangers associated with their dealings. Engaging in trade, negotiation, and the acquisition of rare items, including the essence of individuals, the Dimmers leverage their influence to navigate complex social dynamics and assert control over the regions they inhabit.

Operating with a network of semi-independent members, the Dimmers maintain a presence in several settlements, including Verrow's Span, where they are both recognized and regarded with suspicion. Their influence has been particularly notable during tumultuous times, such as the recent influx of thirty refugees from Dolm's Break, who fled after an assault by the Umbral Beasts. This crisis has provided the Dimmers with opportunities to further their interests amidst the chaos, particularly through the presence of a tall feline katari among the refugees, known for wearing an eye patch and regarded as a semi-independent operator with ties to the faction. Such developments raise questions about the faction's ongoing operations and their adeptness at manipulating local dynamics to their advantage.

The Dimmers also maintain a broader network, as highlighted by their collaborations with Ygva during missions under Grithmaar Deep, where plans were laid to retrieve the Living Fortress and deliver a Blessed Branch to rekindle a pyre at Drosven’s Gate. This underscores the significance of maintaining relationships with various factions. Their operational tactics include infiltration; for example, Draz's engagement with Borek at a communal table served as a distraction, facilitating searches for Heyou's essence. This showcases the Dimmers' intricate social structure and their strategic manipulation for personal objectives. As tensions rise in Verrow's Span, the Dimmers' influence and involvement in local affairs have become increasingly pronounced, manifesting a complex web of alliances and rivalries that they continue to navigate.

Recently, the Dimmers have expanded their influence over Ulmira’s Step, earning a reputation characterized by malignity and mistrust among those in the region. Members have been identified among a group of Umbra-distorted travelers, including Sarru, a dwarf guardian of the Sacred Pyre, and Savel, a giant sorcerer, which has intensified hesitations about confronting them. Their presence is causing local leaders and factions to avoid direct engagement while attempting to pursue powerful artifacts like the Blessed Branch from the Living Fortress. Additionally, the emergence of non-humanoid monsters in Drosven’s Gate signals a significant alteration in the local populace, complicating the situation further and heightening concerns about the Dimmers' reach and the dangers posed by the encroaching Umbra.
