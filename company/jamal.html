Player Character

Jamal

✦ Open the Character Sheet

Jamal, the steadfast dwarf Slayer, is known for his robust build and resilience. He wields an Improved Warhammer, a blunderbuss, and a magical axe, the Axe of Fortunus, reflecting his warrior lineage, and his well-worn armor shows his readiness to confront danger. Jamal is characterized by his action-oriented demeanor, a keen sense of hearing, and extensive knowledge of caves and mining. As a member of The Enduring Lesser Lights, he stands ready to confront threats, showcasing his resilience when he endures a powerful blow from a brute's cudgel but continues to fight back.

During the eruption at the Sacred Pyre in Drosven’s Gate, Jamal swung his warhammer with precision, striking down a Horror and causing additional creatures to manifest. He intercepted an acid jelly with a powerful strike, though the creature's explosion drenched him in corrosive bile. He endured the pain and remained standing, demonstrating his durability. In the caverns, Jamal reacts swiftly to danger, narrowly avoiding an arrow shot by a goblin. He positions himself to meet the charge of the monstrous brute and raises his warhammer for defense. After being struck, he retaliates with forceful blows, coordinating with companions Draz and Heyou to bring down their formidable foe. Their coordinated attacks ultimately lead to the brute's defeat, and Jamal remains resolute as the party debates their next move, agreeing to press on despite the risks.

After the battle at the Sacred Pyre, he inquired about relighting the extinguished pyre and agreed to accompany his companions on a quest to obtain a Blessed Branch for rekindling the sacred fire. He participated in gathering provisions from abandoned stalls, committing to the group's mission as they journeyed toward Ulmira’s Step, ready to face upcoming challenges.

Recently, Jamal has actively participated in strategic ambushes against goblin foes alongside his companions Sylvie, Sarru, Savel, and Yuki. He displays impressive physical prowess, demonstrating agility in navigating hazards and delivering powerful strikes that devastate his enemies. In a pivotal ambush, Jamal takes position as visible bait with his allies, waiting for goblins to approach. When Draz's flaming arrow ignites Flammable Ooze, he engages in combat, strategically navigating a water hazard to deliver a crushing blow that obliterates a goblin. After the initial skirmish, he remains focused on the group's objectives, assessing route options with his companions. Additionally, Jamal encounters the ghost of Mellon, a former companion, on a bridge. Despite his attempts to communicate, Mellon does not acknowledge him, emphasizing the haunting nature of the encounter. Jamal continues to engage in discussions about the party's next moves, weighing the risks of various paths as they prepare for further challenges.

In a recent battle against a Guardian beast, Jamal initially missed an attack but subsequently struck a blow that dealt significant damage, crushing the bones in the beast's paw. Despite taking damage from the creature's retaliation, he mitigated the impact with his resilience and armor. After the beast was defeated, he ensured its death with additional hammer strikes and discovered a Guardian's collar with a broken chain, linking the beast to the Sacred Tree it guarded. Following the encounter, Jamal displayed a practical approach, confirming the creature's demise before proceeding. He later confronted the Askavir, a giant supernatural raptor, with Draz. Despite the fear of the situation, Jamal adapted and survived a fall from the creature, showcasing his toughness and resourcefulness.

Recently, Jamal has been equipped with Blessed Branch, a valuable asset in encounters, and has played a significant role in navigating treacherous mountain paths. He demonstrates adaptability and strategic thinking during a confrontation with the massive witch Ygva, engaging in both melee combat and support roles. In a pivotal moment, he attempts to throw the Blessed Branch to his companion Heyou for safekeeping, reflecting his tactical awareness. However, Ygva intercepts the branches with supernatural speed, forcing Jamal to attack an umbral spirit instead. His attack is effective, dealing substantial damage despite the spirit's ability to partially reform, illustrating his offensive capability and protective instinct for his allies. Later, he utilizes a Guardian's collar, an artifact from a previous adventure, to attack Ygva, inflicting further damage and eliciting a significant reaction from her. His actions are critical during the encounter, contributing to the party's overall strategy against overwhelming supernatural threats. Jamal also attempts diplomacy with Kelun, highlighting his desire for peaceful resolution, though these efforts ultimately prove unsuccessful amidst escalating violence.

In a recent encounter at a witch's camp, Jamal awoke to find a tick attached to his chest, draining his vitality. He promptly struck the tick with his Warhammer while still prone, cracking its hard carapace. Despite the tick draining his strength, Jamal demonstrated notable toughness, mitigating the injury. Sylvie, newly awakened to her magical abilities, cast a spell affecting the tick while Jamal was still engaged in the struggle. Heyou used a Power Push to rip the tick from Jamal's chest and hurled it off a cliff, eliminating the immediate threat. After resting at the witch's camp, Jamal assisted in planning the group's next moves, considering various potential routes and quests. He sampled local fare, including thick worm steaks and powerful freeze-distilled liquor, while interacting with the residents of Verrow's Span. His experiences in the wilderness and adept climbing skills were evident during a challenging ascent, where he used jump-grabs to navigate difficult terrain, showcasing his agility and confidence.

Recently, Jamal engaged in a drinking contest with locals from Simiah at Verrow's Span, but he was defeated when he discovered the locals were cheating by mixing lighter drinks, resulting in him becoming heavily intoxicated and ending up under the table. During a crisis involving refugees from Dolm's Break and the threat of an Askavir, Jamal intervened when the creature captured a refugee in its talons. He struck the Askavir's talon with his hammer, cracking it and forcing the creature to release the captive. In a later confrontation with the grim pyromancer, Jamal executed an opportunity attack with his warhammer, causing the creature to collapse into nothingness. Following the battle, Jamal experienced physical and emotional wounds and expressed uncertainty about the next steps. He participated in discussions regarding the aftermath of the conflict, focusing on the implications of the Askavir’s remains and the need to address the broader crisis affecting the settlement and its new inhabitants.

In the tunnels, Jamal engages with hordes of gremlins, countering their ambush effectively. He crushes five gremlins with his warhammer and provides covering fire against advancing foes. He also conjures a swirling swarm of fireflies to distract the mantises attacking his allies. Additionally, he aids his companions by pulling Sylvie up to secure her footing as the tunnel compresses. His contributions are vital to the group's progress and survival. Jamal is also a skilled tunnel-runner with expertise in excavation and extensive knowledge of underground regions, particularly Grithmaar Deep. His past is marked by trauma from the loss of his village, leading him to develop strong survival instincts and adaptability. While he prefers to avoid Grithmaar Deep due to painful memories, he recognizes its potential for quests that benefit the greater good. His experience in the mountains enhances his skills for navigating dark environments, even amidst his emotional reservations.

Recently, Jamal, a young dwarf of approximately 16 years, has returned to Grithmaar Deep, a dwarven city of many levels. He possesses strong swimming abilities, demonstrated when he swam through an underground cavern and climbed onto his moose mount, You Bastard, while assisting his companion Sylvie. Jamal has a connection to his ancestral heritage, recalling symbols of his people and legendary figures such as Grithmaar The Founder and Tariq The Mason. He expressed confusion about returning to his old home after having fled it barely alive. He recognized a monumental statue marking an entrance to Grithmaar Deep and described the sacred music of the gods that evolved into communal forms in the absence of the deities. During a confrontation with undead dwarves, including friends and neighbors, Jamal experienced a mix of rage and recognition, particularly when he encountered an Undead Captain Of The Guard, a former trainer and friend of his father. He attempted to plead with the undead but was met with taunts and attacks, showcasing his struggle against the memories of his past.

Recently, Jamal has been marked as a heretic, bearing a distinctive mark on his right butt cheek. He undertook a mission beneath Grithmaar Deep to locate a great creature left by Mollthain, the god known as The Rampart, which is believed to be connected to the destruction of his home. He has a practical appearance and actively engages in tasks such as crafting and assisting others. He concealed his heretic mark during his journey to avoid detection. He participated in retrieving a Blessed Branch from the Caranthalis Altar and assisted Heyou in forging a dagger from an Askavir claw. He engaged in conversations about the implications of their quest and overheard discussions about recruitment efforts and concerns over dangers. At one point, he observed a Giant Quadruped and, using his Sound of Silence ability, approached it carefully. He successfully lassoed a Guardian's collar around the creature's neck, attempting to bind it as a companion.

Recently, Jamal, the lone survivor from Grithmaar Deep, wields a new axe in combat and is accompanied by a kalisk mount named You Bastard. He has demonstrated combat skills and leadership within his party. Jamal led the group through a straight passage into the mountain, guiding them down the rightmost path at a fork. He faced a telepathic threat from an iron figure, which stated, “You were among those meant to be destroyed; I will finish the job,” before being impaled and restrained by its spear. Despite suffering bruises and poison, Jamal fought back against goblinoid attackers, killing multiple foes and executing a Death Maneuver that felled three goblinoid minions in a single strike. He contributed to the party's defensive strategies by stacking rubble to create a barricade. While resting, he remained vigilant and detected movement behind the barricade, indicating potential threats. Jamal recognized the being Sylvie described — Mollthain's Warden — as the ancient monster beneath Grithmaar Deep, whose power the party sought for the god pillars.

Recently, Jamal, a resourceful and determined adventurer, is motivated by the recent loss of his family and friends. He seeks thrills and survival in his endeavors. He descended steps to the lake to fish and investigate, retrieving a large broken crab shell with meat inside. He was attacked by several large crabs at the water's edge and witnessed companion Heyou being overwhelmed and torn apart. Jamal fought back using a whirlwind attack to disable the claws of one crab. He captured a live crab underwater, tore off its claws, and fitted it with a collar. He resurfaced riding the newly collared crab and returned it to camp as a new companion.

Recently, Jamal has engaged in stealth approaches, utilizing his blunderbuss in combat. He possesses a one-time use of Mending Touch, a healing ability from the Splendor domain, allowing him to heal a hit point or stress with focused time. During a stealth advance into the tunnels, he evaded an attack from an astral assassin that phased between him and Heyou. He fired his blunderbuss at the astral assassin at close range, but the target was no longer visible when the smoke cleared. Jamal advanced toward a colossal guardian, described as towering like a five-story structure, while Bob the Living Fortress followed behind him. He participated in a coordinated team-up assault with Heyou and Sylvie, resulting in a massive blow that opened a fissure down the guardian's body.

Recently, Jamal intercepted the fleeing towering "dwarf" — in truth a human — whose body shattered into brittle bones, leaving the Axe Of Great Value. He fought the Eldritch Creature alongside Pinchie and Sylvie, suffered a heavy counterattack, delivered a strong follow-up strike while Pinchie held the creature, and administered a healing potion to Heyou.

Jamal is a music lover who possesses a stand-up violin. He attempted to play it in a banquet hall, attracting the attention of Dagga, an antiquarian dwarf, who reprimanded him for using the instrument as a presentation piece rather than for playing. Despite this, they bonded over drinks in a higher-status dining room, where Dagga asked for his loyalty, which he accepted. At the Celsius compound, Jamal conversed with Dagga, sharing stories about climbing the Sacred Pyre and overhearing discussions about an heir. He expressed a desire for drinks and pursued a friendly rapport with her. During an audience with Dean Therion Calanthe, Jamal charged at Therion with his axe but missed and endured multiple attacks from the dean. Dagga was prepared to protect him during the encounter.

Status: Cautiously determined.
