Location

Omer’s Rest

Omer’s Rest is a significant geographical feature marked by a Moon Rune Carving leading into the World Below, characterized by its deep, cavernous structure and inhospitable climate. The landscape around Moon Rune Carving is barren and devoid of life, with remains of large, mantis-like creatures found at the edge exhibiting a partial transformation into stone upon death. The presence of titanic entities, such as a giant titan moving rocks near an abandoned settlement, suggests a connection to the larger ecosystem of the World Below.

Draz, a wilderness tracker, investigated the bodies of the flying mantis-like creatures, noting their phasing qualities and interactions with solid matter. It was discovered that body parts embedded in rock retained their phasing abilities. Sylvie, a witch, surveyed the World Below using her powers, identifying the barren landscape and titan movements. The party communicated with Valghast, a winged lion who revealed connections to a mistress from the sky.

Ygva, a powerful witch with a history of animosity toward the party, aimed to construct a massive pyre to draw the gods down, providing the party with specific leads for their quest. Moon Rune Carving served as a crucial entry point into the World Below, where titans were observed preparing offerings for the pyre, underscoring Omer’s Rest as a gateway to deeper mysteries and potential alliances.
