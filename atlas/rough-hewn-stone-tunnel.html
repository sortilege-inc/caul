Rough-Hewn Stone Tunnel

The Rough-Hewn Stone Tunnel is a dimly lit passage with rough stone walls and a magical light without a visible source. It resembles a corridor leading to unknown depths and is located beneath a structure associated with significant events and notable figures.

Heyou descended into the tunnel with Ygva's unconscious body and encountered Dean Therion Calanthe, who was already present. The tunnel connects to the larger narrative unfolding above.
