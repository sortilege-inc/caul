Dolm's Break

Dolm's Break is a mountain settlement noted for its cliff-hugging architecture, rope bridges, ladders, and carved dwellings. It serves as a connection point for travelers, with paths leading to Tern’s Watch, St. Thera’s Remit, and the ancient city of Amber Reach. The village primarily consists of simiah inhabitants, with a few human and Katari families also residing there. Open plazas and communal spaces accommodate the predominantly simiah population, emphasizing hospitality and togetherness.

However, recent turmoil has shaken the community. Dolm's Break is now known for the chaos that ensued following an invasion by Umbral Beasts, leading to a chaotic evacuation of its residents, primarily goblins and halflings, with some Katari among them. The sacred pyre at the center of the community, which the inhabitants believed was vital to their identity and safety, was extinguished during the turmoil, contributing to the disaster. As the refugees arrived at Verrow's Span, they recounted their dire situation, highlighting the impact of their catastrophic loss.

Dolm's Break features a vibrant community where locals, such as Akiri and Janzi, offer food, drink, and tours of the village. Notable visitors, like Tamber, a Celsian itinerant scholar, share insights about the area, including references to the Celsian Athenaeum and the significance of ancient mountain glyphs, believed to date back to pre-god titan ages. The settlement fosters a blend of hospitality, local culture, and an ongoing exchange of knowledge among residents. The escalation of events culminated in an attack by a large creature known as the Askavir, drawn to the chaos and preying on the vulnerable refugees, further complicating the settlement's plight and highlighting the supernatural threats they now face.
