Location

Thistleglass

Thistleglass is located west of Drosven’s Gate, known for its connection to sacred rituals and the natural world. It is a potential source for a Blessed Branch needed to rekindle the extinguished Sacred Pyre in Drosven’s Gate. The area features a forested environment rich in magical properties, with high-altitude cloud cover that casts a diffused light over the landscape, giving an ethereal quality to the trees. The terrain is characterized by cliffs and snowlines, adding to its mountainous allure.

The urgency to travel to Thistleglass arose after the Sacred Pyre was extinguished, and Sylvie Cerridwen proposed the journey to obtain the Blessed Branch. The group faced a recent eruption of monstrous creatures in the plaza, emphasizing the need to restore the sacred fire. They considered Thistleglass and the alternative destination of Ulmira’s Step, ultimately favoring the closer location. The party prepared for their journey by gathering provisions from abandoned trade stalls, highlighting Thistleglass as a critical point in their quest to protect the town.

In addition to its sacred significance, Thistleglass is associated with witchcraft, evident by the nearby witch's camp, which serves as a shelter for travelers. This camp consists of raised platforms and an open-sided structure under an overhang, providing refuge in the cold climate typical of the mountainous region. The surroundings feature pictographs carved into stone, resonating with the stones of the witch's camp and reinforcing the area's ties to ancient practices. Various creatures inhabit the area, including Feral simiah, contributing to its reputation for both mystical and perilous events.

Recent discoveries reveal that Thistleglass is also a vast forest located near the southeastern coastal tip, associated with the deity Kreth. It is inhabited by larger-than-usual fairies, described as unpredictable and dangerous, prompting nearby communities to avoid the area. The forest is linked to a place known as the Faerie's Guard, where a beast or artifact associated with Kreth may be found. The reputation of Thistleglass instills fear and caution among those who venture near, particularly among crews in Salthewn who deliberately avoid the forest and its fairies. Additionally, Thistleglass has a hazardous coastal environment and treacherous features, further solidifying its reputation as a significant point of concern for travelers, who are warned to avoid encounters with undesirable parties in the area.

Status: To be avoided.
