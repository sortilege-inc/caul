Metal Cell

The Metal Cell is a chamber with solid metal walls, floor, and ceiling. It features a single seamless door seam, indicating a potential exit. The unconscious body of Ygva lies on the ground, suggesting the space may have been used for containment or interrogation.

A hole in the floor leads to a dim, Rough-Hewn Stone Tunnel lit by a sourceless light. This tunnel connects to other areas beneath the Celsian Compound.
