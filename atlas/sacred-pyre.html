Sacred Pyre

The Sacred Pyre is a central feature in the plaza of Drosven’s Gate, known for its solemn atmosphere and reliance on the magical flame that the pyre maintains. This flame is integral to the town's rituals and protective measures. When lit, the pyre provides light and a sense of safety; when extinguished, it plunges the plaza into darkness. The Sacred Pyre serves as a spiritual beacon and a site of power, intended to repel malign forces, particularly those influenced by the Umbra.

A critical event occurred when the pyre went out unexpectedly, leading to chaos as a massive chitinous creature burst forth from the nearby Idol Hollows, initiating a battle in the darkened plaza. The remaining heroes used the high ground of the smoldering embers of the extinguished pyre to gain a tactical advantage. After defeating gelatinous creatures, they determined that rekindling the pyre required a Blessed Branch, as ordinary fire would not restore its magic. Draz informed them that the creatures would regenerate without proper destruction of their remains, underscoring the pyre's significance.

After the battle, the group discussed acquiring the Blessed Branch, planning a journey to Ulmira’s Step while gathering provisions for their departure. The plaza now remains dark and filled with the remnants of conflict, emphasizing the urgency of their mission to restore the Sacred Pyre.

More recently, it has been noted that the Sacred Pyre is located atop a sheer tower within the Celsius compound, emitting a bright light that illuminates the surrounding area. The pyre burns with a unique quality, providing intense illumination with minimal heat. The tower is part of a larger structure that exhibits both maintained and crumbling elements, indicating a once-grand establishment now diminished in scale and occupancy. The compound contains numerous corridors and rooms described as chill, dim, or magically warded, creating a complex environment with varying atmospheres. Recent discussions among guardians about the arrival of "the heir" suggest significant events related to the pyre. The pyre's light serves as a focal point for the compound, with magical emanations concentrated around it, the dean's area, and several workshops. The pyre is integral to the compound's identity, with its flames resonating with the magical energies present throughout Celsius.

The party considered relighting the Sacred Pyre using a Blessed Branch but lacked certainty about the ritual without a priestly guide. Ultimately, they decided to bypass Drosven’s Gate, where the Sacred Pyre had been lost, and continue their journey toward Grithmaar Deep, reflecting on the challenges posed by the Umbra and the Dimmers's influence in Ulmira’s Step.
