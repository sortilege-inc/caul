Glowing Tree

The Glowing Tree is a significant feature within an Underground Complex, known for its radiant golden glow. The environment includes shallow water sections and raised stone platforms, contributing to a mystical atmosphere. The tree is guarded by a powerful, multi-eyed, purple feline-like demon, which holds a Blessed Branch in its mouth. This Guardian is recognized as a malevolent entity, exhibiting intelligence and hostility toward intruders, suggesting it is a corrupted or summoned being. The tree plays a crucial role, as nearby settlements seek Blessed Branch from it.

Evidence of the Guardian's territorial behavior includes an encounter with a Goblin Envoy who offered an alliance to eliminate the Guardian in exchange for safe passage to retrieve a branch from the tree. The goblin warned of the Guardian's lethal capabilities.

The presence of the Glowing Tree and its Guardian represents a significant challenge in the surrounding quest.
