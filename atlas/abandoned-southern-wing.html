Abandoned Southern Wing

The Abandoned Southern Wing is part of the Celsian Compound, featuring a crumbling yet maintained structure. The area is marked by chill and dimness, with many corridors and rooms appearing magically warded. The wing has a smaller student body and staff than in its prime, contributing to a sense of neglect.

Recently, a garrison occupied the Abandoned Southern Wing, raising questions about its current state and purpose. Motifs of crowns and scepters associated with the God-King Otheidas indicate a connection to the past. The presence of the garrison suggests that the wing may still hold strategic or ceremonial importance.
