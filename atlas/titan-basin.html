Titan-basin

Titan-basin is a vast rock formation resembling the roof of a gigantic stone mouth, created by the sliding body of a titan down the mountain. This natural ravine is filled with a river and features a large, cold pool at its base, with waterfalls cascading from either side. The basin's structure suggests an unnatural origin, as if a massive entity had been dragged through the earth, leaving a gouge filled by the river. Mist and slick moss characterize the surrounding environment, complicating traversal.

A cavern behind the waterfall leads deeper into the titan's remnants, where a living tunnel coated in gelatinous sludge can manipulate debris, indicating that the area is inhabited by a living organism. The presence of Ygva, a witch, is felt within the tunnel, suggesting further significance connected to the titan's legacy.
