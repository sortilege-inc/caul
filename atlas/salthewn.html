Salthewn

Salthewn is a coastal port town known for its rugged terrain and syndicate activities. It was the base of the syndicate Heyou once ran with, and its underworld ties run deep. (The giant brothers Ugg and Grug, whom Heyou knew from those days, were muscle for a rival crew, not natives of the town.) The town is defined by its tall spires and distinctive architecture, designed to endure the coastal storms. Its grand structures rise above the landscape, showcasing the ingenuity and resilience of its inhabitants, who have adapted their building techniques to the harsh weather.

Heyou once worked Salthewn with the syndicate he ran with, and his past there surfaced during the chaotic eruption at the Sacred Pyre of Drosven’s Gate, linking the town to broader dangers. The forges of Salthewn are known for a distinctive flammable, kerosene-like substance vital for metalworking — an oily ooze-secretion Heyou later recognized coating the walls of the titan tunnels far to the east, where its ichor caught fire in the dark. The city's impressive skyline, rising on its cliffs above the sea, is visible from the surrounding mountainous country, guiding travelers toward it.
