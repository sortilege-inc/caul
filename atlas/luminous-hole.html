Location

luminous hole

The luminous hole is a prominent geological feature within a cavern, identifiable by a bright light emanating from a hole in the floor that leads down to the ruins of a colossal island city. The environment surrounding it consists of a 20-foot-wide cylindrical passage coated in Living Gelatinous Sludge, which is capable of manipulating embedded debris, suggesting it functions as a single organism. The tunnel constricts gradually, creating an urgent atmosphere as travelers navigate through its organic lining. Stone-bodied sentinels are positioned nearby, guarding the luminous hole.

Interactions with the luminous hole began after a party was swept over a lower waterfall into a frigid pool and discovered a cavern behind a cascade. Draz’s mystical tracking sense revealed that Ygva, their target, lay directly beneath the luminous hole. Examination of the area revealed that they were looking through a hole in the sky at a dead world, with ruins illuminated by a washed-out light source. The scale of the city indicated that its former inhabitants were significantly larger than those exploring the site. Ambushes and hostile creatures posed challenges as the party navigated this space, with notable events unfolding around the luminous hole, including battles and attempts to assess their next steps regarding Ygva.
