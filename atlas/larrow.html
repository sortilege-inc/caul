Larrow

Larrow was once a bustling hub town, the crossroads that knit together the settlements of the region — Vireth Hollow to the southeast, Drosven’s Gate beyond it, and others along the roads it anchored. Then its pyre went out, and no one could relight it.

Some of its people fled; some stayed behind, and the remains of those who stayed can still be seen among the ruins. Larrow is a ghost town now, foreboding and silent, and travelers such as Draz long ago learned to skirt around it rather than pass through. It stands as the region’s plainest warning of what becomes of a settlement whose pyre fails — the fate that now threatens Drosven’s Gate.

Status: A dead-pyre ruin, shunned.
