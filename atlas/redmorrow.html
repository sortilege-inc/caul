Redmorrow

Redmorrow is a coastal community grown up around a scatter of grounded shipwrecks, their hulls long since converted into homes, stalls, and hideaways — a black-market port where goods and people move without too many questions asked. It is among the most populous towns of the region.

It is one of the two coastal footholds of the syndicates, the other being the cliff-carved port of Salthewn where Heyou’s old crew was based. Where Salthewn is all spires and storm-worn stone, Redmorrow is a warren of beached vessels and improvised markets.

Status: A black-market shipwreck port.
