Vireth Hollow

Vireth Hollow is a settlement in the wilds to the southeast of Drosven’s Gate, and the region Draz came from before the road brought him north. It is home to ribbits — the large frog-folk — to fungril, and to a scattering of simiah like Draz himself.

Since the pyre of Larrow went out and the hub town that connected the region fell to ruin, very few people come and go from Vireth Hollow; the road that once linked it outward now runs through a ghost town most travelers avoid, leaving the hollow increasingly isolated.

Status: Isolated since Larrow’s fall.
