Underground Complex

The Underground Complex is an expansive subterranean environment with shallow water sections and raised stone platforms. A distant golden glow emanates from a tree at its center, serving as a focal point. Supernatural threats, including stonewraiths that resist conventional weaponry and retaliate with destabilizing subsonic attacks, lurk within.

The complex features a massive stone creature that emerges from the walls, emphasizing its supernatural nature. Goblin mechanics inhabit the area, highlighting a dual focus on engineering alongside the site's conflicts. A mysterious force in the water can ensnare explorers, adding to the dangers present.

Mellon's ghost reveals the complex's spiritual connections, while an Ancient Spirit warns of a Guardian tethered to the Glowing Tree. This Guardian protects Blessed Branch sought by intruders. Goblins provide insights into the Guardian's behavior and the surrounding territory, indicating a need for strategic planning and alliances.
