Witch’s Mountainside Camp

The Witch’s Mountainside Camp is an open-sided campsite located at a higher elevation, featuring raised platforms and an overhang for shelter from the elements. Fire arrangements at the camp are ordinary, indicating practical use rather than ritualistic. Various witchcraft supplies are present, including cupboards filled with dried berries, powders, leaves, and branches, though there are no maps or notes. Small Pictogram Stones, some arranged on a flat stone, may suggest a connection to the witch's practices or history.

The camp was the site of an ambush by shadowy figures and a giant tick, leading to a confrontation involving spells and physical attacks. Following this threat, the party chose to explore the southwestern path away from the witch and her potential dangers.
