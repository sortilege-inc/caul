Scholar Statue Chamber

The Scholar Statue Chamber is a mixed herbalism and alchemy facility that holds significant importance in the realm of knowledge and healing. At its heart stands a striking statue of three Dwarven women scholars, symbolizing Vigor, Valor, and Trust. This statue is located in a drier side hall, where meditating at the statue of Vigor offers a slight restoration of vitality to those who seek its blessings.

The chamber is equipped with planters, scroll racks, a mortar and pestle, and trays filled with trace powders and tools, creating an atmosphere rich with the potential for both study and experimentation. However, the cultivated plants within the study have begun to go feral, hinting at a loss of control over the once-tended space. A box containing concentrated chemical powders, discovered by Draz, releases noxious fumes that induce coughing and stinging eyes when opened, adding an element of danger to the chamber.

Intruders to the Scholar Statue Chamber face a formidable guardian: a large undead dwarf wielding an epic battle-axe. This spectral foe challenges those who enter, and during a confrontation, he is ignited by a flaming arrow, retaliating with a heavy crossbow that strikes the statue's eye. In a display of his power, he summons spectral lieutenants, escalating the threat within the chamber.

Recently, Draz's exploration revealed the hazardous nature of the chemical box, which he managed to close and take with him, suggesting that the chamber holds more secrets yet to be uncovered.
