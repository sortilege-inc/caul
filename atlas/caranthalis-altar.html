Caranthalis Altar

The Caranthalis Altar is a significant religious site dedicated to the deity Mollthain, known as The Rampart. It features sacred architecture and houses a Blessed Branch, essential for rituals and offerings. The altar serves as a place of worship and a source of divine connection for the local populace. Historically, it is linked to divine activities associated with the god-king Otheidas and contains remnants of offerings and relics from past worshippers, such as circlets and other items connected to the gods.

Recently, Ygva provided a Blessed Branch from the Caranthalis Altar, designated for use at a sacred pyre at Drosven’s Gate, with the stipulation that it must remain there once ignited. The importance of the altar and its offerings was underscored by the group's efforts to secure the branch for their mission while respecting the altar's significance.
