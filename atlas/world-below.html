World Below

The World Below is a cavernous realm accessible through a Moon Rune Carving, characterized by a barren expanse devoid of life. This landscape features large stone-like structures and titanic beings, including automatons and titans engaged in construction activities, such as arranging stones for an immense pyre. The climate is desolate, permeated by a sense of foreboding and ancient power. It serves as a convergence site for mystical energies and remnants of the gods, with ongoing plans to draw divine beings from the upper world.

Draz, a wilderness tracker, investigated remains of large, flying mantis-like creatures at Moon Rune Carving's edge, noting their bodies partially phased into stone. The party discussed options for descent, weighing resting versus crafting gliders from the creatures' wings. Sylvie, a witch, observed a titan moving a massive rock near an abandoned settlement through Moon Rune Carving. After signaling the titan with a fireball, a winged lion named Valghast responded, landing at Moon Rune Carving and inquiring if the party were "servants of my mistress," leading to a meeting with Ygva, a powerful witch seeking remnants of the gods to bind them in the World Below.

Ygva revealed her intentions to use god-touched artifacts and assistance from titans to restore balance to the upper world. The party agreed to assist her and descended into the World Below, landing on a massive tower roof. They observed titans constructing the pyre and preparing offerings. Ygva requested the Askavir Heart from Heyou, indicating its significance in her plans. As the party navigated the altars and artifacts, they discussed potential quests to retrieve god essences, emphasizing the urgency of their mission.
