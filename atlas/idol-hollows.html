Idol Hollows

The Idol Hollows is a geological feature adjacent to the central plaza of Drosven’s Gate, characterized by cracked earth that serves as a source for grotesque creatures. The area is known for erupting hostile entities, including gelatinous shapes that ooze from the ground and can split and regenerate unless properly dealt with.

A significant incident occurred when the Sacred Pyre in the plaza was extinguished, plunging the area into darkness. This event was followed by the emergence of a massive creature from the Idol Hollows, resulting in a chaotic confrontation with various horrors. The group recognized the necessity to relight the Sacred Pyre, which required a Blessed Branch, leading them to consider potential retrieval locations such as Ulmira’s Step. The encounter underscored the ongoing threat posed by the creatures of the Idol Hollows, indicating the need for further action.
