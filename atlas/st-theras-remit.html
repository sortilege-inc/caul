St. Thera’s Remit

St. Thera’s Remit is a northwest monastery located in a mountainous region, near the ancient city of Amber Reach. It serves as a pilgrimage site and a center for knowledge, likely housing scholars and providing spiritual guidance. A sacred pyre in the area suggests the presence of ritualistic practices.

The nearby community comprises predominantly simiah, along with a few humans and Katari families, highlighting its diversity. The village of Verrow's Span functions as a hub for travelers to the monastery, with local inhabitants indicating routes leading to St. Thera’s Remit. This connection underscores the monastery's significance as a destination and a resource for the villagers.
