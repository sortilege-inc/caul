Location

sheltered cleft

The sheltered cleft is a cavernous space once utilized by Stone Weavers, providing protection from the elements. It remains intact and has not been used for some time. The cleft offers a safe haven for travelers, allowing them to rest away from immediate threats.

The cleft was discovered by a group after arriving at the Stoneheart Peaks, where they made camp. One member reshaped the stone to close the cleft entrance, ensuring safety during the night.
