Moon Rune Carving

The Moon Rune Carving is situated in a mountainous region, roughly five miles from a notable path adorned with large pictographs standing 20 to 30 feet tall. This carving is etched into solid rock, without any evident cave or entrance. The rugged terrain of the area shows no signs of active maintenance, suggesting the carving has existed for many years. It is associated with Ygva, a witch, indicating its potential historical or cultural significance. The climate around the carving is cold, approaching the snowline, reflective of the harsh mountainous environment.

The carving is the mark Sylvie recognized from the Pictogram Stones taken at the witch's camp — a moon-sign set into the high road with no door beneath it, one sign in the coded sequence the mile-high Mountain Pictographs repeat. It leads nowhere and opens nothing; its meaning is unread.
