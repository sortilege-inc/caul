Location

dead world

The dead world features the ruins of a colossal island city surrounded by water and illuminated by a washed-out light source in the sky. The architecture indicates inhabitants of giant stature, with structures disproportionately larger than those familiar to visitors. The city is in a state of decay, lacking vegetation or life, suggesting it has been abandoned for a long time.

Access to the dead world is through a hole in the sky, creating a stark contrast between the vibrant world above and the desolate landscape below.

Draz’s mystical tracking sense points downward, indicating the presence of the witch Ygva within the city. Navigating the treacherous terrain and height, the environment poses challenges in finding a safe route to the ground while remaining aware of their direction toward her location.
