Location

Ygva’s Peak

Ygva's Fire is a notable location defined by a mystical fire linked to Ygva, a towering 7.5-foot witch. The area includes a padded bedroll, indicating its use for rest and potential healing. This site is significant for supernatural encounters and cosmic revelations regarding reality and the influence of powerful entities. The surrounding environment conveys an otherworldly atmosphere where life and death intersect, allowing for profound transformations.

Access to Ygva's Fire involves navigating treacherous mountain paths and crossing a rope bridge affected by supernatural forces. Upon arrival, the party encountered Ygva, who teleported into their midst, leading to a violent confrontation. Ygva captured Sylvie and transported her to Ygva's Fire, where she regained consciousness to find Ygva. In this moment, Ygva divulged cosmic truths about their world being an "egg" for departed entities and presented Sylvie with an ultimatum concerning her magic. The encounter intensified when Draz attempted a rescue, resulting in chaos during their conversation and culminating in Ygva extracting Sylvie's heart and taking several Blessed Branch, marking a moment of transformation and loss at Ygva's Fire.
