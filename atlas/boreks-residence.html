Borek’s residence

Borek's residence is a large, multi-unit house located in Ulmira’s Step, associated with Borek, a fixer for the Syndicate known as the "Syndicate." The structure contains several apartments, including a six-bunk bedroom where three bunks are occupied, each with a small locked chest. The residence is cluttered, filled nearly floor-to-ceiling with various items such as crates, statues, armor, weapons, and tools, indicative of a hoarder's stockpile.

Borek's authoritative presence creates a tense atmosphere, compounded by the threats from the Syndicate. The residence features a dark hallway that contains a magical warded door that can be bypassed. Evidence of criminal activity includes Borek confronting Draz about his connections to a group using explosives, which escalated tensions. A binding rune on Draz's arm was revealed, linking him to Borek and Syndicate. Following a chaotic confrontation, valuable items were retrieved during the escape, including a prosthetic leg and a fear-inducing dagger.
