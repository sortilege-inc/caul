Celsian Compound

The Celsian Compound is the campus and headquarters of the Celsian Athenaeum — the grounds the party reached by teleport, colloquially "the Celsius." It is an old structure that has been maintained despite its age. It features a large Sacred Pyre burning atop a sheer tower, casting light over the area. The compound includes student barracks, dining areas, and various corridors and rooms that are chill, dim, or magically warded. A notable sealed, vaultlike door is present near the entrance hall, resonating with magic and under the dean's purview. The compound is situated on a slope, with buildings connected by stone hallways resembling castle battlements.

Visitors must contribute artifacts to the treasury upon arrival. Dagga Brunnvar greets newcomers and provides information about the compound. The Sacred Pyre and various magical emanations are notable features. Strong Splendor emanations are clustered around the temple, the dean's area, and several workshops. Dean Therion Calanthe oversees the compound, and Inola, who radiates Splendor, is also present.
