Tlellic’s Altar

Tlellic’s Altar is located within a vast cavern in the World Below, characterized by eight large flat stones arranged in a circular formation. This site is crucial for rituals aimed at summoning gods, where remnants and totems of deities are gathered to attract divine attention. Surrounding the altar, offerings such as blessed branches, weapons, jewels, and carvings are collected in preparation for a colossal pyre designed to draw the gods back to the realm.

The altar serves as a focal point for rituals, highlighting the importance of balancing the offerings among the gods to ensure their responses. The strategic arrangement of these offerings indicates careful planning for the summoning process.
