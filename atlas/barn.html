Barn

The Barn is a structure within the Celsian Compound, where the Keeper Of Splendor conducts her work. It is a site for magical activities, particularly the preparation of meals. The Barn is associated with Inola, a middle-aged Simiah who radiates strong magical energy known as white-hot Splendor. It serves as a retreat from the busyness of the compound, providing a quieter space away from the dean and students.

Acolytes escorted Sylvie to the Barn, mentioning that the Keeper was performing ablutions there. Upon entering, Sylvie encountered Inola, who welcomed her warmly and invited her to dinner. The Barn is characterized by a sense of magical energy due to Inola's presence as a source of Splendor.
