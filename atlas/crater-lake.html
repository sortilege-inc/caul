Location

crater lake

The crater lake is located within a bowl-like depression atop a mountaintop in the Stoneheart Peaks. It has formed from rainwater and is surrounded by rough stone steps leading down to its edge. The water emits a sulfurous smell, and crabs inhabit the area. The crater appears to have been shaped by a significant event, with remnants of ancient villages and houses now crumbled and washed away.

A colossal, grimacing face-like impression is visible on the far side of the crater, resembling ancient idols. Newly cut steps lead down to the lake. The area is home to aggressive crabs. Distortions in the environment occur when magic is cast, causing the ground and sky to bulge toward one another. A sheltered cleft nearby provides a location for camping.
