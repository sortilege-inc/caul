Sacred Tree

The Sacred Tree is a mystical entity, once vibrant and blessed, which now appears burned and regrown, exhibiting brittle wood and signs of destruction. Its bark and branches show charring consistent with a history of damage.

The tree served as a protective entity for a Guardian beast, which was physically and mystically linked to it; evidence of this connection is a Guardian's collar found after the beast was defeated. Surrounding the tree are tight, rugged oval seed containers, suggesting potential for the growth of similar sacred trees in the future.

The tree's condition was investigated after the defeat of the Guardian beast, which had been vanquished using Explosive Barrels and magical force. A Blessed Branch carried by the beast emitted a brief warmth, indicating continued divine power, and the party harvested three additional branches. However, the return of goblins with explosives led to a catastrophic detonation, resulting in the complete destruction of the Sacred Tree.
