Amber Reach

Amber Reach is an ancient city built into the rocky mountains, featuring intricate stone architecture primarily suited for simiah inhabitants, alongside a few human and Katari families. The settlement has around thirty residents and serves as a hub for trade and cultural exchange, particularly with itinerant scholars like Tamber, who educate local children. The climate is mountainous, near the snowline, with occasional avalanches.

Verrow's Span, a cliff-hugging village, connects to Amber Reach. Locals greet newcomers with food and drink, showcasing their hospitality. The unique architecture is tailored for simiah physiology. Routes leading to various locations, including Tern’s Watch and St. Thera’s Remit, highlight Amber Reach's significance, with connections to pre-god titan ages and mysterious glyphs carved into the mountains.

The Amber Reach, located to the west of Verrow's Span, is tied to Draz's family history. Draz's parents and twin siblings sought refuge there during times of hardship, making this region significant for him, representing memories and connections related to his familial ties and abandonment. The elder of Verrow's Span shares that Draz's parents intentionally left him behind in the Wilds as they traveled west, reflecting a past filled with difficult choices and sacrifices. This area serves as a pivotal point of origin for Draz's lineage.

More recently, Amber Reach has been noted for its connection to a significant past and rich lore. It features a sumptuous chamber with a long table and windows that evoke a sense of beauty. The area is linked to the Heart of Stone, associated with a Runt God that failed to ascend, drawing power from the location. The Amber Reach includes a torn mountaintop where something massive had been ripped free. This mountaintop served as a vantage point from which Sylvie spotted Kaelyn and Rhys in the distance, accompanied by an eldritch entity. The group chose to remain at this location, which Rhys identified as the safest he knew, and took a long rest there. Sylvie recognized the table and windows from her dream as part of the Amber Reach, leading her to consider attempting to teleport to that place.

Tamber provides insights into a magical necklace and Rune-Marked Box discovered by Sylvie and Heyou, revealing a map of lands beyond their previous knowledge. Amber Reach is recognized as a critical point of cultural and historical significance. The community is known for its unique cuisine, including worm steaks, and fosters connections beneficial for travelers.
