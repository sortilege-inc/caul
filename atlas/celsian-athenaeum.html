Celsian Athenaeum

The Celsian Athenaeum is a prestigious college and library located in the far west, known as an ancient repository of knowledge and artifacts. The name refers both to the institution and to its campus and headquarters — the walled Celsian Compound (colloquially "the Celsius"), set in the Amber Reach. It serves as a center for education, attracting scholars and students who seek to explore its extensive resources on history, magic, and ancient civilizations. Vast halls filled with preserved treasures and remnants of divine significance create an academic atmosphere where scholar-like academics share their insights, enhancing the understanding of the world's lore. The Athenaeum is dedicated to the preservation of knowledge and artifacts, emphasizing a philosophy of safeguarding wisdom rather than exploiting it for power. This ethos is exemplified by the expulsion of Ygva, a former initiate who practiced "depletion," a technique that drains the essence of entities to enhance magical effects.

Significant to the party's journey is Tamber, a tutor connected to their quest and a Celsian Itinerant. She has a history with Ygva, having trained together at the Athenaeum, which underscores the tension between her destructive practices and the Athenaeum's preservationist ethos. Locals recognize the Athenaeum's importance in understanding regional history and ancient symbols believed to date back to the age of titans, marking it as a notable point of interest. The influence of the Celsian Athenaeum is apparent as the party navigates the ongoing crisis involving Ygva and the Askavir, while Tamber retains knowledge and techniques that Ygva did not learn, indicating a potential advantage in future encounters.

Recent developments indicate that Ygva intends to raid the Athenaeum, seeking god-touched artifacts that are crucial for her objectives. Her plans highlight a growing tension, as she perceives the Athenaeum not merely as a repository but as a potential source for gathering powerful treasures linked to the gods. The implications of her actions position the Celsian Athenaeum as a critical site for both preservation and potential conflict, with treasures meant for observation now at risk of being exploited for more nefarious purposes.

The Celsian Compound

The Celsian Compound is the campus and headquarters of the Athenaeum — the grounds the party reached by teleport, colloquially "the Celsius." It is an old structure maintained despite its age, featuring a large Sacred Pyre burning atop a sheer tower that casts light over the area. The compound includes student barracks, dining areas, and various corridors and rooms that are chill, dim, or magically warded. A notable sealed, vaultlike door sits near the entrance hall, resonating with magic and under the dean's purview. The compound is set on a slope, its buildings connected by stone hallways resembling castle battlements.

Visitors must contribute artifacts to the treasury upon arrival; Dagga Brunnvar greets newcomers and provides information about the compound. Strong Splendor emanations cluster around the temple, the dean's area, and several workshops. Dean Therion Calanthe oversees the compound, and Inola, who radiates Splendor, is also present.

The Barn

The Barn is a structure within the compound where the Keeper of Splendor conducts her work — a site for magical activities, particularly the preparation of meals, and a quieter retreat away from the dean and students. It is associated with Inola, a middle-aged Simiah who radiates the white-hot Splendor. Acolytes escorted Sylvie here to meet the Keeper, who welcomed her warmly and invited her to dinner.

The Abandoned Southern Wing

The Abandoned Southern Wing is a crumbling yet maintained structure, marked by chill and dimness, with many corridors and rooms appearing magically warded. The wing has a smaller student body and staff than in its prime, contributing to a sense of neglect.

More recently, a garrison occupied the wing, raising questions about its current state and purpose. Motifs of crowns and scepters associated with the God-King Otheidas indicate a connection to the past, suggesting the wing may still hold strategic or ceremonial importance.

The Audience Hall

The Audience Hall is a prominent chamber within the compound, known for its grand architecture and the presence of multiple copies of Dean Therion Calanthe during gatherings. It serves as a venue for important discussions and decisions regarding The Elect and their potential ascension. The atmosphere is charged with magical energy, featuring various powerful entities, including the Keeper Of Splendor and a fungal priest.

During a notable event, the party entered the Audience Hall with the unconscious body of Ygva, where they encountered Dean Therion and other influential figures. The dean questioned the party's intentions concerning Ygva and the Sacred Pyre, leading to a tense exchange about ascension and the fate of The Elect. The hall experienced a sudden escalation into conflict, with magical attacks and strategic maneuvers among the gathered individuals; a deep rumble was felt through the floor as the situation intensified.
