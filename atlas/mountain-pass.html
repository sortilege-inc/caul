Mountain Pass

The Mountain Pass is a rugged terrain characterized by steep cliffs and narrow pathways, surrounded by towering rock formations. The climate is often harsh, with sudden weather changes creating thick cloud cover. This pass serves as a strategic route, frequented by creatures such as goblins and other supernatural entities.

The area is marked by remnants of past conflicts, including Flammable Ooze harvested from fallen foes. Supernatural occurrences, including the voices of spirits and ghostly figures, contribute to the region's foreboding atmosphere. An Underground Complex within the pass features shallow water and a Glowing Tree, leading to encounters with stone creatures and a Guardian beast.
