Grand Hall

The Grand Hall is a vast ceremonial space within Grithmaar Deep, featuring impressive pillars and statuary that honor past leaders. The hall is currently flooded, with water levels rising to shoulder height, indicating a significant disturbance in the area. Statues commemorate notable figures, including Grithmaar The Founder and Tariq The Mason, underscoring its historical importance as a place of governance and celebration.

The hall is inhabited by undead dwarves, including familiar faces from Jamal's past. The flooding environment poses hazards, and the presence of a Dwarf Colossus and spectral foes adds to the threats faced within the hall.
