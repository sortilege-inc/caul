Audience Hall

The Audience Hall is a prominent chamber within the Celsian Compound, known for its grand architecture and the presence of multiple copies of Dean Therion Calanthe during gatherings. It serves as a venue for important discussions and decisions regarding The Elect and their potential ascension. The atmosphere is charged with magical energy, featuring various powerful entities, including the Keeper Of Splendor and a fungal priest.

During a notable event, the party entered the Audience Hall with the unconscious body of Ygva, where they encountered Dean Therion and other influential figures. The dean questioned the party's intentions concerning Ygva and the Sacred Pyre, leading to a tense exchange about ascension and the fate of The Elect. The hall experienced a sudden escalation into conflict, with magical attacks and strategic maneuvers among the gathered individuals. A deep rumble was felt through the floor as the situation intensified.
