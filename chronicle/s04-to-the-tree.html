To the Tree

Session 04·2025-09-07

Summary

The case: we’re deep in the Mountain Pass, facing slime creatures and goblins. The stakes? Our lives and a missing friend, Mellon, who vanished when he passed insubstantially through a goblin rockfall, leaving his cat Yuki behind.

  • Regroup after the skirmish; Draz recounts the chaos, barely dodging a goblin archer.
  • Heyou proposes a tactical ambush: he and Draz on the cliffs, Jamal, Sylvie, Sarru and Savel, and Yuki as bait.
  • Ignite Flammable Ooze from the ogre’s corpse for a signal.
  • Thick clouds roll in, darkness swallows the pass; Sylvie and Jamal hear whispers of the dead, Sylvie catches her grandmother’s voice.
  • A rockfall strikes; Draz dodges, Heyou absorbs the blow with Sylvie’s scarf.
  • Five goblins, including a leader, stumble into the kill zone; Draz fires a flaming arrow, causing an explosion that scatters the foes.
  • Sylvie unleashes lightning, obliterating targets; Jamal crushes a goblin, Draz finishes the last one.
  • The party weighs route options; they split: Draz and Heyou take the upper path, Jamal and Sylvie with Sarru go lower.
  • Draz spots Goblin Engineers; a stone creature emerges but misses Heyou.
  • They find an underground space with a golden glow; Draz’s arrow fails against a Stonewraith, which retaliates, forcing him down.
  • Heyou uses Rain of Blades to dispatch goblin mechanics.
  • Sylvie gets caught in water, something grips her ankle; Mellon’s ghost appears to Jamal but won’t communicate.
  • Sylvie frees herself and initiates peace with a Stonewraith; Heyou activates ancient tech, allowing a spirit to inhabit him.
  • The spirit warns of a powerful Guardian tied to the Glowing Tree we need to reach.
  • We reaffirm our goal for Blessed Branch, knowing the Guardian awaits; take a short rest.
  • Heyou builds rapport with the goblins, securing a Satchel Of Explosive Powder and a promise of more if we take down the Guardian.

Costs: injuries from the ambush, the ghost of Mellon haunting us, and the looming threat of the Guardian. Loose ends: goblin alliance uncertain, the Guardian still a mystery, and Mellon’s fate unresolved.
