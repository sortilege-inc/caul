Trail of the Witch

Session 09·2025-11-07

Summary

We’re up against Ygva, the Umbra-wielding witch. She’s got a reputation for destruction, and we’re here to stop her before she wreaks more havoc. The plan’s simple: a fake heart to distract her while Heyou, the clank rogue, keeps the real one hidden and invisible. We take the mountain path, knowing a direct descent would be a climb from hell. Draz tries to get the locals at Verrow's Span to help, but they’re too busy with their own crises to care about Ygva. Meanwhile, Jamal loads up on rations and ale, bracing for a long haul.

  • Draz consults Tamber, who spills the beans on Ygva’s expulsion from the Celcian Athenaeum and warns that his amulet won’t protect against her Umbra magic.
  • We start our descent, using ropes while Heyou belays from above, keeping his distance like a shadow.
  • We spot the coastline and some Salthewn architecture, but the river ravine below looks wrong—like something big got dragged through the earth.
  • At a mountain river, we devise a plan to cross using a log and ropes. Heyou pumps up Jamal and Draz before we take the plunge.
  • Sylvie crosses the log but gremlins ambush us—one’s flinging rocks at her while another targets Jamal.
  • Jamal goes to town, taking out five gremlins, and Sylvie incinerates most of a horde with Fireball.
  • As more gremlins jump across, Draz shakes off a sneak attack, while Heyou stays hidden, clutching the real heart.
  • Heyou finishes off the last gremlin, but Sylvie spots others above, smashing a barricade and unleashing a torrent down the ravine.
  • We’re swept over the falls; Heyou tries to save us but can’t reach in time. We land in a cold pool and swim to a ledge leading to a cavern.
  • Draz senses Ygva’s path leading into the cavern, while Heyou arrives with a splash, still unseen.
  • Inside, we find a living tunnel coated in sludge that starts to close in on us. Draz pushes forward, but Heyou gets trapped.
  • Sylvie blasts the organism with Fireball, freeing Heyou, and we push deeper into the tunnel.
  • We reach a chamber with stone sentinels near a bright hole. Sylvie commands one to stay put and awakens the Living Fortress.
  • Heyou pilots the Living Fortress, clearing our way until we hit a hooded figure on a narrow bridge.
  • Looking down through the hole, we see ruins of a colossal island city, a dead world illuminated by a pale light.
  • Two giant mantis-like flyers attack Draz, injuring him, while an Astral Assassin appears beside Heyou.
  • Heyou stabs the assassin, but it’s Jamal who conjures fireflies to harry the mantises while Sylvie tries to Siphon Essence, only to drain herself instead.
  • The Living Fortress crushes one mantis, but the pressure’s on. Heyou invokes The Heart’s power for a devastating smite that takes out the assassin, leaving him unconscious.

We’re in a tight spot. Heyou’s down, and we need to decide if we summon Ygva or risk the descent through that glowing hole. The costs are piling up, and loose ends are hanging like smoke in the air.
