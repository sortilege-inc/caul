Grithmaar Deep's Shadowed Threats

Session 14·2026-01-16

Summary

The case: We’re deep in the mountain, heading toward Grithmaar Deep, with Jamal, the last of his settlement, recounting how organized raiders wiped out his people. They were ready for vermin, not a full-on assault. We’ve got dragon bile glands stashed in the Living Fortress and dragon scales from our last haul.

  • Rested in a straight antechamber, tension thick in the air.
  • Jamal led the way, torch in hand, while Heyou cloaked him in Invisibility.
  • At a three-way split, Jamal picked the right path, but a tremor shook the ground.
  • A rope trap triggered, debris rained down, injuring Jamal and Kalisk.
  • The Living Fortress couldn’t pass the rubble; the rest of us squeezed through.
  • Heyou tried to clear the blockage, but only stirred up dust; he commanded the Living Fortress to bulldoze it.
  • An armored figure emerged from the dust, attacking the Living Fortress with a barbed spear.
  • Draz climbed up, shot an arrow at the figure’s knee, but it bounced off; goblinoid spearmen struck him.
  • Heyou slipped past the debris, took out one goblinoid, while Sylvie’s magic failed to connect.
  • A second horned figure charged, spearing Jamal; chaos erupted as Draz scrambled for a shot.
  • Sylvie protected the Living Fortress with magic, while Heyou broke a chain, freeing it.
  • Jamal cleaved through goblinoids, Draz shot the iron figure in its visor, and Sylvie cursed it.
  • Sylvie froze the iron figure; Draz removed its helmet, revealing Splendor magic inside.
  • We dismantled the suit, stacked rubble for a defensible rest site.
  • Jamal heard movement behind our barricade; enemies were lurking.
  • Sylvie sent her Umbral Raven to scout; it spotted Guardian below, but it got destroyed.
  • Sylvie reached out to her unseen patron in a dream for guidance on gaining power from the Beast.
  • Draz and Heyou captured a goblinoid for interrogation while Jamal fought off a counterattack.
  • Sylvie woke, eliminated two enemies, but the captive goblinoid recoiled in fear, calling her a witch.
  • Her attempt to siphon essence failed, killing the goblinoid instead.

Costs: Jamal took poison, and we’re still sitting on a pile of rubble with Guardian ahead. Loose ends: We need to capture Splendor and figure out how to channel that essence into the Living Fortress.
