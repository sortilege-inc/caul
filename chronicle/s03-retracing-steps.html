Retracing Steps

Session 03·2025-08-13

Summary

The Enduring Lesser Lights found themselves back in Ulmira’s Step, a settlement nestled in the bones of a titan. Unease hung in the air like smoke; the pyre sputtered low, and whispers of vanished delvers filled the corridors. We needed answers, and fast.

  • Sylvie pushed for a visit to the witch at the high shrine, desperate for a blessed branch to reignite Drosven’s Gate’s pyre.
  • Heyou hinted at secrets Sylvie was keeping, suggesting another route.
  • Sylvie clammed up, revealing only that the witch drew from the Umbra and would want a price.
  • Savel, Mellon, and Sarru joined us as we geared up, Sylvie casting wards and knitting a scarf for Heyou—promising him socks next.
  • We descended into the crevice, the air thick with kerosene, the light from the pyre dwindling.
  • Draz spotted a dark slick on the walls, a flammable ooze—danger lurking like a snake in the grass.
  • An arrow whizzed past Jamal, and the ambush was on.
  • A hulking goblinoid brute charged, its cudgel swinging; Jamal took a hit and went down.
  • Heyou struck, igniting the ichor from the brute’s wound, lighting up the passage.
  • Sylvie shielded us from the flames as the battle raged; Draz dispatched a goblin that hit Sylvie from behind.
  • We struck back: Jamal hammered, Heyou snuck, and Sylvie unleashed blasts.
  • Draz’s arrow exploded the ooze, but the brute reanimated, fueled by the Umbra.
  • Mellon broke ranks, sprinting deeper into the crevice, triggering an avalanche that sealed his fate.
  • Draz tried to climb up, but arrows rained down, and he took hits.
  • We regrouped, spotting goblins fortifying a kill-corridor, abandoned lookout posts hinting at a trap.
  • Sylvie warned against pushing further, but we pressed on, resolve hardening as we prepared to confront the goblins.

The cost? Jamal down, Draz injured, and Mellon’s fate hanging in the balance. Loose ends? The witch's price loomed large, and the deeper shadows of the Umbra awaited.
