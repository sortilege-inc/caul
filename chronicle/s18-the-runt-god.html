Session

The Runt God

Session 18·2026-03-12

Summary

In the aftermath of the eruption at the Sacred Pyre, we found ourselves up against Spectral Dwarven Guardians and a towering figure swinging the Axe Of Great Value.

  • Draz took the high ground on a statue plinth, nailing the axe-wielder in the grip with a precise arrow.
  • The spectral guardians tried to close in, but Draz held firm, unleashing Fireflies to scorch nearby foes.
  • As the axe-wielder made a break for it, Draz shot him in the knee, slowing his escape.
  • Sylvie, Jamal, and Heyou arrived just in time; Jamal charged in and tackled the enemy, shattering him into brittle bones and leaving the Axe Of Great Value behind.
  • With Jamal’s giant crab, Pinchie, we climbed the torn mountaintop, spotting Kaelyn and Rhys in the distance, a massive eldritch entity looming behind them.
  • Heyou moved in on Kaelyn while Sylvie confronted her about a past Midsummer solstice rite gone wrong, learning that Rhys looked undead and had sent us to the higher realm with the Runt God.
  • Suddenly, Sylvie found herself ensnared by Kaelyn, while the eldritch creature blasted her with magic, leaving her severely injured.
  • In the chaos, two Stone Elementals flanked Rhys, who called for help. Draz fired his blunderbuss at the creature, but it absorbed the shot like a sponge.
  • Reality warped around us, leaving us “unstuck from reality.” Sylvie and Jamal tried to coordinate an attack, but it fell flat. The creature retaliated, landing a heavy blow on Jamal.
  • Pinchie charged in, grappling the creature and dealing significant damage. Jamal followed up with a strong strike while Rhys shot a magical beam at the creature, but the Stone Elementals blocked Heyou’s search for a physical “leash.”
  • Draz slid beneath the creature and fired into its gills, scoring a hit, but it plunged into the ground, severing Rhys’s beam.
  • Jamal rushed to give Heyou a healing potion. Rhys approached Sylvie, confirming she was okay and expressing his belief that the world was dead. He urged us to find the Runt God, claiming Kaelyn’s power had kept us alive.
  • We agreed to stay put, which Rhys said was the safest spot he knew, and settled in for a long rest.

During the rest, Rhys reflected on his past, admitting he had followed Kaelyn blindly and described Sylvie’s previous response as transformative. He explained that in the upper world, they drew power from the Heart of Stone, the Runt God—too weak to ascend until Sylvie had thrust it upward. Draz pushed for saving our current world, while Heyou backed a plan to draw power back down. Despite our differing views, Rhys remained set on finding and wrangling the Runt God to facilitate ascension.

Seeking guidance from Ygva, Sylvie slipped into a deep sleep, straining to make contact. In her dream, she awoke in a lavish chamber, dressed by attendants, and took a seat beside another figure at a long table, where petitioners sought counsel. Sylvie recognized the locale as somewhere within the Amber Reach to the west, concluding she could attempt to teleport to that place.

Costs: injuries sustained by Sylvie and Jamal, loss of the Axe Of Great Value, and the uncertainty of our next move. Loose ends: the fate of the Runt God, the implications of Kaelyn’s control over the eldritch creature, and the potential teleportation to the Amber Reach.
