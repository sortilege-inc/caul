Mollthain's Protector, Suffused in Splendor

Session 15·2026-01-30

Summary

After a brief respite in the underground caverns, we—Sylvie, Draz, Jamal, and Heyou—geared up to plunge deeper into the treacherous tunnels. We were on a mission to uncover the lurking threats that had been reported by Sylvie's spectral raven.

  • Sylvie, now an essence-siphoning vampire mage, flexed her newfound powers after draining an enemy dry.
  • In a dream, Ygva, her distant patron, gifted her the spell Damnation, heightening the stakes of our mission.
  • We named our construct ally, the Living Fortress, "Bob," and Heyou led us stealthily into the shadows.
  • Forewarned by Sylvie, we detoured to avoid a Flaming Skull sentinel, assessing the narrow bridges ahead.
  • Noticing an Acid Bile Sack from Bob, we kept it in mind as a potential breaching tool.
  • Our Compass steadied, pointing at “11 o’clock,” a welcome change from its previous erratic behavior.
  • We retraced our steps to confront the Flaming Skull, where Sylvie detected dangerous levels of Splendor magic in the area.
  • To protect us, she applied a timelock effect on the Flaming Skull, freezing it until our next rest.
  • Two astral assassins phased in, targeting us; one marked Draz for death, ignoring his armor, while Jamal narrowly missed a shot.
  • A colossal guardian approached, towering like a five-story structure, radiating overwhelming Splendor energy.
  • Draz communed with the guardian, learning it was placed there by Mollthain to steward a local light.
  • Our fireball attack failed to faze the guardian, which retaliated by raising a crystalline wall, separating Draz from the group.
  • Draz scrambled up to the guardian’s shoulder, while Sylvie power-pushed the wall, creating rubble for movement.
  • One Astral Assassin fell under pressure, but the guardian rejected our request to stand down, shaking the chamber.
  • Draz lost control, entering a lucid ancestral-dream state as Bob hurled acid bile at the guardian, carving deep grooves.
  • The guardian wept molten streams, forming a protective barrier, igniting under a subsequent fireball.
  • We executed a coordinated strike, opening a fissure that vented a geyser of luminous liquid.
  • In a climactic moment, Sylvie activated Damnation, sacrificing her own health to absorb the geyser's power, coating herself in radiant energy.

Costs: Draz took severe damage from the assassin's mark. Sylvie sacrificed health to absorb the geyser's power. Loose ends: The Flaming Skull remains frozen, and the guardian still stands, a looming threat in the shadows.
