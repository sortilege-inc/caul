Witch's Peak

Session 06·2025-09-18

Summary

The party's on a tightrope above a bottomless abyss, hunting for answers in a world that’s gone dark. The mountain paths are treacherous, and the air crackles with supernatural tension. We’re on the brink of something big, but we can’t afford to slip.

  • Sylvie and Heyou start crossing a rope bridge, feeling the tremors of Askavir's return as its wings beat the air, shaking the bridge beneath them.
  • A Ghostly Specter, eyeless and translucent, blocks Sylvie’s way. She asks politely for passage, and it steps aside, no hostility in its movements.
  • Kelun splits off to announce our arrival, hinting at the formalities we’re about to face. Heyou seeks Sylvie’s insight on our fate; she grimly notes we might be freed or meet dire consequences.
  • Ygva, a towering witch, teleports in, shattering any hopes of diplomacy. With a flick of her wrist, she unleashes a spiritual attack that leaves us gasping.
  • Ygva summons five misty entities to strike at us, but their incorporeal blows fall flat. Jamal throws Blessed Branch to Heyou, but Ygva intercepts them, gaining the upper hand.
  • Draz takes aim, unleashing a bow attack that obliterates the remaining Umbral Spirits and wounds Ygva. Jamal seizes the moment to strike with a Guardian's collar, further injuring her.
  • In a desperate move, Ygva teleports away with Sylvie, leaving us in chaos.
  • Sylvie awakens beside Ygva, who reveals our reality was an "egg" for cosmic entities now gone, leaving decay. Ygva offers Sylvie a choice: give up her magic or face captivity.
  • Draz, frustrated, attacks Ygva, but she retaliates by tearing out Sylvie’s heart and vanishing with the Blessed Branch.

The cost? Sylvie’s heart is gone, and we’ve lost all our Blessed Branch. Yet, against the odds, Sylvie sits up, alive but changed, with a gaping wound in her chest. Draz steps back, horrified. Sylvie speaks into our minds, revealing her new Dread domain powers, siphoning healing instead of channeling it. Heyou reattaches his old leg, which now has a mind of its own. The mission failed, but the story’s just getting started.
