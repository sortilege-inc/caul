Session

A Piece of Sky

Session 17·2026-02-27

Summary

The case was simple enough: escape Grithmaar Deep, but things never go as planned. We were stuck in a flooded mess, and our You Bastard was drowned.

  • Navigated a stone staircase from the flooded pool to an alternate mountain exit.
  • Found the kalisk dead and Bob, the stone golem, trapped underwater.
  • Set up camp on the staircase, letting the floodwaters settle.
  • Heyou dove back underwater, snagged the Blessed Branch and the Guardian's collar, but Bob stayed immobile.
  • Sylvie tested her teleportation ability, blinking us to a cratered mountaintop in the Stoneheart Peaks.
  • Spotted a colossal face pressing into the sky before it vanished, a memory from Jamal's childhood.
  • Sylvie explored the ruins, noting newly cut steps leading to a lake.
  • Jamal descended, retrieved a broken crab shell, and provoked a swarm of large crabs.
  • Heyou blasted crabs at close range; Sylvie unleashed destructive magic, cooking the crabs, leaving one declawed to scuttle back.
  • Felt a strange bulging sensation in the ground and sky as we harvested crab carcasses, realizing they would regenerate if not processed.
  • Found a sheltered cleft used by Stone Weavers; Heyou closed the entrance, and we settled in for the night.
  • Sylvie and Jamal reassembled Heyou with a healing draught; Sylvie had unsettling dreams of disembodied voices and former associates.
  • Jamal captured a live crab, fitted it with the collar after a fierce underwater struggle, and resurfaced riding it.
  • Sylvie warned us to give her space as she attempted to restore the mountaintop, engaging in risky rites over the following nights.
  • Witnessed a gigantic presence pressing against the sky with growing urgency.
  • On the final night, Sylvie enlisted Heyou’s help, and a massive stone body tore through the sky, plummeting toward us.

Costs: We lost our You Bastard, faced the threat of regenerating crabs, and Sylvie’s dreams hinted at dangers lurking in the shadows. Loose ends: Jamal’s new crab companion, Sylvie’s ongoing rites, and the colossal stone mass descending above us.
