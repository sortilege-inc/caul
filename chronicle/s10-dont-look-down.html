Don't Look Down

Session 10·2025-11-21

Summary

In the depths of the World Below, we, The Enduring Lesser Lights, took on a heavy case. Ygva, a powerful witch, needed our help to gather remnants of the gods. The stakes? The upper world’s ruin loomed over us like a dark cloud.

  • Draz examined the remains of two massive flying mantis-like creatures, noting their phasing properties.
  • We hatched a plan to carve off phased body parts, eyeing the wings for potential gliders.
  • Heyou suggested we experiment on-site while we weighed our options for a rest.
  • Sylvie, attuned to the Scarred Earth, spotted a titan moving a massive rock near an abandoned settlement.
  • She tried to communicate with the titan using her Voice of Dread, but it didn’t respond.
  • To grab attention, she cast a fireball as a flare, summoning a winged lion to our ledge.
  • The lion landed with a thunderous roar, and Draz interpreted its speech, asking if we were servants of its mistress.
  • Ygva arrived, riding the winged lion, greeting Sylvie as “sister” and revealing her plan to gather godly totems.
  • We accepted Ygva's proposal to descend into the World Below, witnessing titans preparing a colossal pyre.

The costs? Heyou was tasked with presenting the Askavir Heart to Tlellic’s Altar. Ygva aimed to amass artifacts from eight gods, and as we passed the altars, Heyou recognized an arm-band bearing his sigil, hinting at the creation of a new being. Draz offered his compass, pointing toward Ossa, a skyward target. Jamal hesitated about Grithmaar Deep due to his past but ultimately agreed for the greater good. Sylvie raised concerns about the implications of the pyres being consolidated below, hinting at dire consequences for the communities above.

We stood on the precipice of a new quest, burdened by the weight of our choices and the looming threat of Ygva’s ambitions. Loose ends? Plenty.
