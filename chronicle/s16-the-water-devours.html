The Water Devours

Session 16·2026-02-13

Summary

The case: Sylvie, Draz, Jamal, Heyou, and Bob—their automaton ally—found themselves in a tight spot when the cavern floor gave way. They plunged into a dark, water-filled abyss, and the stakes were high.

  • Sylvie and Jamal surfaced, while Heyou and Bob struggled with their instinct to breathe.
  • Sylvie climbed onto Bob's head, keeping above the rising water; Heyou swam to her side and used Healing Hands to restore her vitality.
  • Draz summoned healing lily pads, buoying the party as the water surged.
  • Jamal mounted You Bastard, hauling Sylvie up as they swam toward potential exits, while Draz lit a torch to pierce the darkness.
  • A spectral dwarf appeared; Draz shot an arrow, slowing it but not stopping its advance.
  • The ghost shrieked, chilling Draz as it drifted toward Heyou, who dodged the attack thanks to his auditory safeguards.
  • Jamal spotted a monumental statue marking the entrance to Grithmaar Deep, realizing they had fallen into the outskirts of the dwarven city.
  • Sylvie's Umbral Raven reconstituted, providing a brief moment of comfort as they entered a vast ceremonial hall, water threatening to engulf them.
  • Undead dwarves, including Jamal's friends, screamed at Sylvie but hesitated upon seeing him; Sylvie's radiant aura unsettled them.
  • In a desperate move, Sylvie tried to siphon essence from the undead but failed, leading to several clawing at her before recoiling from her light.
  • She unleashed a Power Push, clearing space as a surge of water forced them deeper into the hall.
  • Jamal was attacked by a Dwarf Colossus, a towering amalgamation of undead dwarves, restraining him amid the flood.
  • In a moment of desperation, Sylvie successfully cast Siphon Essence, draining the animating umbra from a dozen undead, including Jamal's former crush, which enraged him.
  • Jamal punched Sylvie, fracturing their party dynamics as the water continued to rise.
  • Heyou hurled himself against the heavy rear doors, forcing them shut against the incoming wave, buying them time.
  • The doors buckled under pressure; Sylvie attempted to freeze the flood into Ice Spikes, inadvertently striking Heyou.
  • The forward doors blew open, and the surge flushed an undead dwarf down the next passage, dropping the water level to Sylvie's shoulders.
  • Jamal confronted an Undead Captain Of The Guard, pleading in Dwarven but receiving taunts and real blade strikes in return.
  • Draz discovered a side hall with a statue of three Dwarven women scholars and meditated to restore vitality, but a box of Noxious Powders released fumes, causing him to cough.
  • The largest undead dwarf Draz had ever seen challenged him to a duel, landing a heavy blow before Draz retaliated with a flaming arrow.
  • The flaming giant summoned spectral lieutenants, swelling the enemy presence as water continued to rise.
  • Heyou sealed the rear floodgate with Sylvie's help, but water began forcing in from other passages, threatening to trap them.
  • Jamal dove into the flooded residential quarter for keepsakes but realized he would drown if he stayed.
  • Adrenaline kicked in; Jamal swam back upstream to rejoin Sylvie near the doorway to safer passages.
  • The party regrouped and swam toward Draz's dry corridor, making steady progress despite the current.
  • Sylvie's Umbral Raven scouted ahead, leading them toward a Scholar Statue Chamber where Heyou gathered useful tools while scanning for threats.

Damages: Tensions flared between Jamal and Sylvie, and the undead threat loomed larger. Loose ends: The party still faced rising water, spectral foes, and the need to regroup and strategize for their escape.
