The Long Way Around

Session 11·2025-12-05

Summary

The party gathered under Ygva’s watchful eye, ready to hunt down the great creature left by Mollthain, the god known as The Rampart, beneath Grithmaar Deep. This beast had ties to the ruin of Jamal’s home, and we needed answers.

  • Sylvie flashed her circlet, a connection to the god-king Otheidas, and Ygva let it slide.
  • We needed a Blessed Branch, essential for our mission, to keep the flames of a sacred pyre alive.
  • Heyou handed Ygva pinecone-like seeds; she accepted them, setting the stage for a trade.
  • Ygva gave us a Blessed Branch from the Caranthalis Altar, but we had to promise to replace it if she failed to cultivate it.
  • We stashed the Blessed Branch in the Living Fortress, courtesy of Heyou’s Phasing Vambraces.
  • Found a blunderbuss-style handgun in the relics; Draz and Heyou teamed up to craft more ammo.
  • Ygva offered to make us spectral to slip through rock to Grithmaar Deep, but we decided to make some stops first.
  • She whisked us up through a "hole in the sky" to a ledge, then to the top of a waterfall, clearing our path from the tunnels.

Once outside, Ygva pointed us to a cut-stair route down to the cavern, starting at an old temple near Tern’s Watch. Valghast would wait for us, and Ygva promised to send dreams to Sylvie. Our mission was clear: recover the Living Fortress, deliver the Blessed Branch to reignite the pyre at Drosven’s Gate, and head to Grithmaar Deep.

  • Heyou took the reins of the Living Fortress, our ride across the surface.
  • We hid our heretic marks, avoiding recognition as we reached Verrow's Span, where the refugees were stabilizing.
  • Secured the body of Askavir, tied to the departed god Tlellic, for our examination.
  • Heyou picked claws, beak, and select feathers for crafting, while Sylvie eyed Askavir’s eyes for her bag.
  • Heyou forged a dagger from one of the claws, with Jamal lending a hand, while Draz collaborated with locals for more blunderbuss ammo.

We took two long rests at Verrow's Span, regrouping and working on personal projects. Sylvie knitted socks for Heyou and started a sweater for Draz. Draz deepened his bond with Lila and her grandchild Bufa, future guides through ruins. Sylvie’s meditation sparked vivid dreams, stirring memories with Ygva and her origins.

  • We debated three routes but chose to retrace our steps through the mountains, steering clear of Thistleglass.
  • Draz tried to recruit Tamber, a Celsian academic, for our quest to revive the Old Gods, but she shot him down, fearing for social stability.
  • Sylvie enforced secrecy about our plans, twisting Draz’s arm to keep it under wraps.

Our journey wound along carved ledges and runes, but a blizzard hit us hard. Sylvie cleared a tunnel with her magic. Heyou piloted the Living Fortress through the storm, pressing on as the night wore thin. At dawn, Jamal rallied us, and Sylvie cast a Wall of Flame for warmth.

We camped near ancient titan ruins outside Ulmira’s Step, plotting our next move. Heyou recounted dealings with Borek, a flashy Dimmers member, and we hatched a plan: Draz would distract while Heyou and Sylvie went invisible to find Heyou’s essence.

  • Jamal spotted a massive, moose-like quadruped and crept closer, latching a Guardian's collar around its neck, hoping to make it a companion.

Costs: uncertainty about the Blessed Branch’s fate, lingering doubts about our next steps, and the weight of secrets we carry. Loose ends: Tamber’s refusal, the need to secure our plans, and the looming shadow of Grithmaar Deep.
