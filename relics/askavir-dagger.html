Askavir Dagger

The Askavir Dagger is a weapon crafted from the claw of an Askavir, featuring a curved point. It has the unique property of causing stress to those it strikes.

During a confrontation with a dragon, Heyou attempted to backstab the creature with the dagger, but the attack failed to penetrate deeply, resulting in him being briefly lifted off his feet as the dragon thrashed. The dagger's effects were further noted when used alongside the spell Blades Everywhere, which rained blades upon clustered foes, highlighting its potential to impact the mental state of its targets. Additionally, the dagger is connected to the Devour Ring, suggesting it may possess further properties or enhancements related to that item.
