Phasing Vambraces

Phasing Vambraces, magical item. These vambraces enable the wearer to phase objects through solid matter.

Heyu utilized the Phasing Vambraces during a mission in Grithmaar Deep to conceal a Blessed Branch inside the Living Fortress, making it undetectable. This was crucial for rekindling a sacred pyre at Drosven’s Gate. The need for the Blessed Branch arose after acquiring it from Ygva, who stressed the importance of having a replacement if her cultivation failed. Heyu's decision to carry the branch was based on his stealth capabilities.
