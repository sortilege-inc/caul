Axe Of Great Value

The Axe of Great Value is a large weapon associated with a towering foe described as a human, often mistaken for a dwarf due to his size. It is heavy and difficult to handle, particularly for the wielder, who struggles to lift it after sustaining injuries. The axe is rumored to possess great worth.

During an encounter, Draz shot an arrow that impaired the wielder's hands, making it difficult for him to wield the axe effectively. As the battle progressed, the wielder attempted to retreat while dragging the axe behind him. After a series of attacks, including a shot to the knee that slowed the foe, Jamal executed a takedown, resulting in the foe shattering into brittle bones and leaving the Axe of Great Value behind. The party recovered the axe from the remains of the wielder.
