Fortunas

Fortunas is a magical axe currently wielded by Jamal. It deals 2D10 plus 8 magical damage, providing a higher guaranteed floor of damage compared to Jamal's Improved Warhammer.

Heyou transferred Fortunas to Jamal after a series of intense battles against various foes, including a dragon. Jamal now prepares for further challenges as the group navigates dangerous territories.
