Demon Beast Collar

The Demon Beast Collar is a formidable artifact that played a crucial role in the confrontation between Jamal and Ygva, a powerful witch. Its exact origins and inherent powers remain shrouded in mystery, but its use as a weapon suggests it possesses capabilities that can influence even the most formidable entities. During a pivotal encounter, Jamal wielded the collar to inflict 23 damage on Ygva, which prompted her to retaliate. This moment underscored the collar's significant impact on the battle's outcome, as Ygva subsequently seized Sylvie and teleported away, highlighting the collar's importance in the dynamics of their conflict.

The collar itself is a substantial metal piece, discovered on the guardian beast after its defeat, featuring a broken chain that indicates the creature was once physically tethered to a sacred tree. This bond hints at both mystical and material connections, suggesting a deeper significance to the guardian's existence. The collar serves as a trophy of victory for Jamal and raises questions about the forces that bound the guardian to its post. The presence of the beast's cold, viscous fluid instead of blood further emphasizes its supernatural nature, making the collar a critical clue in unraveling the relationship between the guardian and the sacred tree.

Current developments reveal that the collar's influence extends beyond mere physical damage; it symbolizes the dual nature of the beast's confinement and serves as a key artifact in understanding the broader mystical forces at play in the world.
