Ancient Tech Rune Tiles

Ancient Tech Rune Tiles are inscribed with runes linked to unknown technologies and ancient practices. Stolen from Ygva's camp, they are believed to hold significance associated with the remnants of the past.

During a camping night, Sylvie attempted to study the tiles but was unable to unlock their function. As the party journeyed back along a marked path with similar runes toward Grithmaar Deep, Sylvie continued to seek to understand their purpose. The exploration of the environment revealed carvings that matched the tiles, suggesting a connection between the tiles and their traversed locations.
