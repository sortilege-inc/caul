Blunderbuss Handgun

Blunderbuss Handgun

The blunderbuss handgun is a unique firearm that resembles a traditional blunderbuss. Draz discovered this weapon among relics and it initially came with four shots. The weapon requires the user to possess the knowledge or resources to craft additional ammunition for continued use.

Draz found the blunderbuss handgun during an exploration with assistance from Heyu, who provided smithing insight. Following its discovery, Draz expressed intent to craft more ammunition and collaborated with villagers to create four additional shots, including one special round.
