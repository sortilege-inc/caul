Axe of Fortunus

The Axe of Fortunus is a Tier 3 weapon discovered in a cache room containing various items, including armor and tools. It is secured by Sylvie for delivery to Jamal, indicating its significance in their endeavors.

During the heist at Borek's residence, Sylvie located the Axe of Fortunus while navigating through a stockpile of goods. After securing the weapon, Sylvie and Heyou exited Borek's building with the Axe of Fortunus, Heyou's missing leg, and the Devouring Dagger. They planned to rendezvous with Jamal outside Almira’s Step, solidifying the Axe's role in their escape and future plans.

Heyou transferred the axe to Jamal after a series of intense battles, including one against a dragon. Jamal now wields it, dealing 2D10 plus 8 magical damage — a higher guaranteed floor than his Improved Warhammer.
