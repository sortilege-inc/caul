Goblin Black Powder

Goblin Black Powder is a volatile explosive substance derived from goblins, known for its significant impact in combat scenarios. This black powder was notably utilized by the character Heyou during a fierce encounter against Ygva, a formidable witch. When combined with a torch thrown by Draz, the black powder resulted in a powerful explosion that inflicted 34 damage on Ygva and several umbral spirits. The event showcased the potential of coordinated attacks to penetrate supernatural defenses, although it also had unintended consequences, as the explosion caused Sylvie to fall unconscious, complicating the battle's dynamics.

In addition to its combat applications, Goblin Black Powder is often stored in a satchel, which was provided by a group of goblins as a gesture of goodwill during negotiations. This satchel contains materials intended for defensive measures, specifically for constructing an exploding bridge. The goblins' willingness to share their explosive powder reflects their engineering culture and practical approach to warfare, highlighting the importance of alliances and resource management in tactical preparations for future confrontations.

Current developments reveal that the goblins have promised additional supplies of black powder contingent upon the successful elimination of a guardian beast, further emphasizing the strategic role this substance plays in the ongoing conflicts and alliances within the game world.
