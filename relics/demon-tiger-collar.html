Demon Tiger Collar

The Demon Tiger Collar is a unique item made from an exceptionally tough, compressed plant-fiber weave. It features intricate hidden stitching that reinforces a sense of ownership when fastened onto a creature, suggesting a binding effect between the wearer and the owner.

The collar, while strong, can be cut through with the appropriate technique. It is believed to be designed to indicate ownership, allowing the user to establish a bond or companionship with another creature when placed around its neck.

Status: Active.
