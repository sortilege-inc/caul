Improved Warhammer

The Improved Warhammer is a formidable weapon wielded by Jamal, a skilled dwarf warrior, renowned for its enhanced destructive capabilities. This weapon is designed for devastating melee combat, delivering significant physical damage with a damage roll of 2D12 plus 6. Its extraordinary power was vividly demonstrated during a combat encounter where Jamal executed a critical hit that obliterated a goblin, leaving only shattered remains in its wake.

In a notable ambush against a group of goblins, Jamal effectively crossed a water hazard before utilizing the Improved Warhammer for a decisive strike, which underscored its significance as a tool for destruction and contributed to the party's tactical success. The warhammer's might was further showcased during a roadside confrontation, where Jamal performed a double front-flip dismount from his Kallusk mount and crushed the remaining horde with a single blow. Additionally, he struck a dragon during an ambush, playing a crucial role in its defeat. The weapon's ability to deliver crushing blows, snapping bones and inflicting severe damage on foes, solidifies its reputation as a key asset in battle.

Status: Active.
