Kalisk’s collar

Kalisk’s collar is a magical item associated with a Kalisk, a creature found drowned. The collar was recovered by Heyou during an underwater expedition, alongside a Blessed Branch.

Jamal fitted a live crab with the Kalisk’s collar after capturing it in a crater lake. He removed the crab's claws and attached the collar, resurfacing while riding the collared crab back to camp.
