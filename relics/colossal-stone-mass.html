Relic

colossal stone mass

The colossal stone mass is a massive entity resembling a celestial body, descending rapidly toward the mountain. It features a grimacing face-like impression, akin to behemoth-sized versions of ancient idol faces. This visage was observed pressing into the sky before vanishing, indicating its immense size and presence.

During Sylvie's attempts to restore the mountaintop, she felt the ground and sky bulge toward one another and glimpsed an immense hand pressing into the sky. This phenomenon occurred each time she worked magic, suggesting a connection between her efforts and the colossal stone mass. On the final night of her attempts, as she sought the power to restore the peak, the stone body tore through the sky above and began plummeting toward their location.
