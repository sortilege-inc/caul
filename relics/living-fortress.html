Living Fortress

The Living Fortress is a massive, sentient stone construct, an ancient automaton the party bound to their service with a Stoneweaver focus. This giant walking construct serves as a mobile stronghold, capable of phasing through obstacles to traverse difficult terrain while concealing its contents. The Living Fortress acts as both a means of transportation and a protective barrier against environmental threats, providing shelter and safety to those within.

The fortress features a tree atop its structure, the only greenery in the surrounding environment, symbolizing life amidst the harsh landscapes. During their journey through the mountains, the party engaged the Living Fortress when Heyou took control, utilizing its capabilities to navigate treacherous paths. The fortress provided essential shelter from severe weather, acting as a windbreak against a blizzard. It also demonstrated its capacity to store valuable materials, such as volatile bile sacs extracted from a dragon's corpse, showcasing its role in safeguarding precious items.

Heyou utilized phasing Vambraces to store a Blessed Branch inside the fortress for concealed transport. The group relied on the fortress's size and capabilities to facilitate safer traversal of deep snow and dangerous paths, with members riding on it during their journeys.

Current developments reveal that the Living Fortress continues to be a central asset for the party, not only for travel arrangements but also for its ability to carry and safeguard valuable materials, reinforcing its importance in their adventures.
