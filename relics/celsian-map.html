Celsian Map

The Celsian Map is a grand cartographic representation of extensive lands, revealing regions far west beyond known territories. It serves as a vital tool for navigation and understanding geography, marking towns and significant locations. Its intricate symbols and pictographs suggest a complex history, possibly dating back to the age of titans.

The map is first revealed by Tamber, a scholar in the village of Verrow's Span, who emphasizes that the known portion of land is merely a fragment of the larger world. This revelation prompts discussions on potential future paths, highlighting the map's importance in the quest.
