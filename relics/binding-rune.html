Relic

binding rune

The binding rune is a coercive symbol located on Draz's arm, recognized by Borek for enforcing a group bond among those who bear it. Borek suggests that the rune may also have memory-clouding effects, significantly impacting the mental state of its bearer. Such marks are typically employed when individuals require coercion to collaborate, indicating a manipulative aspect of its magic.

During a confrontation, Borek forcibly reveals the rune, emphasizing its importance and warning Draz of the potential consequences. Borek expresses his disapproval of dark magics, preferring illusions over coercive forms. He hints that Draz may have lost critical memories due to the rune's effects, indicating its ability to obscure significant moments in his past.
