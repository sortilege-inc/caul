Pictogram Stones

The Pictogram Stones are a set of 23 small, flat stones, each about the size of a mouse, adorned with various pictograms. Four stones were arranged on a larger flat stone, indicating a deliberate pattern. They were discovered by Heyou in a witch's camp in the mountains.

The stones matched a sequence found in large pictographs embedded in the mountain, which were observed along the upper road. These pictographs, measuring 20 to 30 feet tall, appeared every mile. Notably, the Compass of Draz reacted to the pictograms, suggesting a connection to the environment. The arrangement of the stones may relate to the history and practices of the area, linked to ancient glyphs and a bygone era.
