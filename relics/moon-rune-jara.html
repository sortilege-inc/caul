Moon Rune Jara

The Moon Rune Jara is a powerful and unsettling artifact closely tied to the witch Ygva, who resides near a collapsed mountainside shrine. This rune serves as both a symbol of Ygva's authority and a conduit for her magic, drawing from the mysterious Umbra rather than the more commonly known Aetherweave. Its significance was first revealed to Sylvie, a member of the Lesser Lights, through vivid visions that underscored its importance to her quest.

During a critical moment at Ulmira’s Step, the discovery of the Moon Rune Jara led to a pivotal realization for Sylvie, as locals confirmed its deep connection to Ygva. This revelation heightened the urgency for the party to obtain a blessed branch, which is exclusively available from the witch herself. Sylvie, however, cautions her companions about the potential costs associated with the Moon Rune Jara, hinting at the heavy price Ygva may demand for her assistance.

As the adventurers traverse treacherous caverns, the Moon Rune Jara remains a silent yet significant element of their journey, embodying the unknown consequences tied to Ygva's magic. Current developments indicate that Sylvie is deliberately withholding the full implications of the artifact from her companions, suggesting a deeper and more complex relationship with the witch's powers than initially understood.
