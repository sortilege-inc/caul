The False Heart

The False Heart is a crafted object used by the party to deceive Yigva, an Umbra-wielding witch. It serves as a fake heart intended to lure Yigva into a trap, while the real heart is kept by Heyou, who must remain invisible and distanced from the confrontation. This distinction between the fake and real heart is vital to the party's strategy.

The heart possesses latent abilities that can be invoked in moments of necessity. Heyou uses its power during a critical confrontation, unleashing a devastating smite that destroys an Astral Assassin. However, this act of drawing on the heart's strength causes Heyou to collapse unconscious, underscoring the heart's dual role as both a tool for deception and a source of incredible power.
