Small Iron Lockbox

Iron Lockbox

The small iron lockbox is a tightly sealed container with a keyhole, adorned with strange, unfamiliar runes that suggest it may have significance beyond its physical form.

  • Discovered by Heyou at a witch's camp among other items.
  • Heyou emphasized its value to his companions and contemplated its potential worth based on his syndicate background.
  • He maintained possession, ensuring no one else took the lockbox as the group planned their next moves.

Status: In possession of Heyou.
