GM Note

Draft — Sylvie Cerridwen

Working character notes and mechanical scaffolding.

☾
Behind the Veil — GM material. Spoilers, draft notes, and cosmology that is not yet canon (and may never be). Players of the Enduring Lesser Lights, turn back.

Name: Sylvie Cerridwen

Ancestry: Human

Age: Early 40s

Class: Witch (Wizard)

Community: Ridgeborne (specifically, from the now-destroyed Stoneheart Peaks) Specialization: School of Knowledge

Core Concept: Sylvie was a Stoneweaver from an isolated, mountain community dedicated to containing an ancient magical nexus. A profound conflict with a powerful colleague, whose insidious magical manipulations escalated into an attempt to dominate and violate her magical channels, led to a cataclysmic confrontation. This event, known as the "Sundering of the Stoneheart," resulted in the destruction of her home and the irreversible transformation of her magic from earth-attuned to lunar. She now wields potent, moon-infused magic, bearing the indelible marks of this destructive transformation.

Daggerheart Mechanics:

  • Hope: To find a new purpose where her unique understanding of magic can be valuable, and to prevent widespread destruction or imbalance caused by unchecked power.
  • Fear: That the power she was forced to unleash will consume her, or that she might inadvertently cause similar widespread devastation.
  • Experiences:
  • Sister Moon: Reflects a heightened magical potency under direct moonlight, allowing her to push limits or perceive hidden truths related to lunar energies.
  • The Scarred Earth: Represents the profound loss of her home and her intuitive understanding of corruption, destruction, or fragmented magic, particularly regarding geological or elemental forces.

Key Background & World-Building Elements for GM Agreement

The following elements form the foundation of Sylvie's character and would need to be integrated into the game world's lore and geography.

  • The Stoneheart Peaks & The Ridgeborne Community: An ancient, isolated human community nestled deep within the mountains. This community, specifically the Stoneheart Peaks settlement, is now destroyed/sundered/ruined due to a cataclysmic magical event.
  • The Stoneweaver Order & The Earth-Heart:
  • The Stoneweavers: An ancient, hereditary magical order within the Stoneheart Peaks. Their magic was traditionally earth-attuned, focused on harmony, containment, and subtle manipulation of mountain energies.
  • The Earth-Heart: A profound, ancient, primal nexus of magic hidden deep within the Stoneheart Peaks. It is a vast, powerful entity whose immense, raw energy requires constant guardianship.
  • The Five Guardians: Only five Stoneweavers (Sylvie, Kaelen, Rhys, and two others) were entrusted with its guardianship, performing the Confluence Ritual to contain its volatile power.
  • Kaelen & Rhys:
  • Kaelen: A powerful Stoneweaver whose ambition led him to seek absolute control over the Earth-Heart. He engaged in insidious magical and psychological manipulations directed at Sylvie, attempting to destabilize her connection to the Earth-Heart and gain dominance.
  • Rhys: A Stoneweaver and Sylvie's peer who, despite understanding Kaelen's problematic actions, chose to prioritize Kaelen's promises of power for the order over intervening to support Sylvie, remaining silent and complicit.
  • The Sundering of the Stoneheart:
  • The Event: A cataclysmic magical confrontation during the sacred Confluence Ritual. Kaelen's escalating magical assault and his attempt to directly control and violate Sylvie's magical channels triggered Sylvie's forceful, newly awakened lunar magic.
  • The Cataclysm: The resulting clash of unleashed power between Sylvie and Kaelen, combined with Kaelen's previous manipulations and the compromised state of the Earth-Heart, caused the Sundering of the Stoneheart, destroying the mountain settlement (and much of the mountain itself). Occasional survivors may be encountered. The surviving members of her community, if any found her, would likely view her as a destructive force, a pariah responsible for their ruin.
  • The Fates of the Five Stoneweavers:
  • Kaelen: Was utterly obliterated by Sylvie's power, leaving no trace.
  • Rhys: Was trapped within the fractured remnants of the Earth-Heart in a unique magical stasis/prison, a living consequence of his choices.
  • The Other Two: Were lost to the immediate cataclysm and are presumed dead.
  • Sylvie's New Magic (Lunar Weaving) & Magical Items:
  • Shift in Power: Sylvie's old earth-attuned Stoneweaver magic was irrevocably severed. Her new power is entirely lunar-based, manifesting from a profound connection to the "Sister Moon."
  • The Staff of Sundered Moonlight: Her magical two-handed staff was forged in the chaos of the Sundering. An ancient tree branch, steeped in the Peaks' energy, became infused with her raw lunar magic, transforming into a staff dense as petrified wood, pulsing with inner moonlight and silver veins.
  • The Whispering Deck: A cherished tarot deck, a gift from her grandmother (a previous Stoneweaver). Sylvie had it on her person during the Sundering, and it was imbued with the chaotic lunar energies, becoming a living link to her past and a transformed magical artifact. It is used for meditation and unlocking magical insight.
  • Knowledge Acquisition: She studies moon magic through meticulous direct observation of the moon and magical experimentation, compiling insights into her grimoire.  She also seeks out forgotten lore scraps, insights from oral history traditions, ancient runes from forgotten days, trying to piece together what the wild magic is that speaks to her now.
  • Flint: Sylvie's crow familiar. He served as a silent witness during the time of Kaelen's manipulations and now remains her loyal companion.
  • Sylvie's Lunar Weavings: She knits, especially under moonlight, weaving silent protective spells and lunar blessings into the fabric. These charm-infused knitted items are often given to those she perceives as vulnerable.

Mechanism Explaining Sylvie's Moon Tie: A Convergence of Blood, Lore, and Cataclysm

Sylvie's profound and sudden connection to the Sister Moon is not a singular phenomenon but the culmination of several interwoven threads: a deeply ancient, almost forgotten aspect of Stoneweaver lore; a specific, powerful ancestral heritage; and the cataclysmic trauma of the Sundering itself.

  • The "Sister Moon" in Ancient Earth-Heart Lore (Earth-Heart's True Nature): While the dominant Stoneweaver tradition focused solely on the Earth-Heart's terrestrial power, deeper, more archaic texts within their sacred lore spoke of a complementary force: the "Sister Moon." This wasn't merely the celestial body, but a recognized cosmic counterpart to the Earth-Heart's primal energy, representing balance, cycles, unseen currents, and receptive power – an ethereal yin to the Earth-Heart's grounded yang. Over millennia, as the Stoneweavers became increasingly pragmatic in their containment duties, this lunar aspect faded into obscured metaphor, honored in name but rarely truly understood or actively engaged. It was a concept, a philosophical balance, rather than an accessible magical wellspring.,
  • An Ancestral Lunar Heritage (Bloodline Revelation): Generations ago, Sylvie's grandmother's mother brought a unique thread into the Cerridwen lineage when she married into the clan. She hailed from a hidden, ancient tradition (perhaps from a nomadic people, or a valley clan that revered celestial bodies), carrying a potent, latent affinity for lunar magic. This affinity was passed down through the generations, a quiet, almost imperceptible hum in the bloodline, lying dormant within most of her descendants, including Sylvie's grandmother and mother, as it lacked the specific trigger or environment to manifest. It was a seed that never fully bloomed within the earth-attuned environment of the Stoneheart Peaks.,
  • The Catalyst of Cataclysmic Trauma (Latent Affinity Activated & Earth-Heart's Rebalancing): Sylvie herself, unbeknownst to her, carried this intensified, latent lunar affinity in her blood. Her own quiet, intuitive nature, while perfectly suited to earth-weaving, also inherently resonated with the subtle, introspective power of the moon. The truly transformative moment came during the Sundering. Kaelen's relentless, aggressive, and violating magic didn't just clash with Sylvie; it assaulted the very balance of the Earth-Heart itself. His attempts to dominate and force the ancient nexus, coupled with his profound violation of Sylvie's own magical channels, threw the entire system into an unprecedented, catastrophic disequilibrium. When Sylvie's traditional earth magic was irrevocably severed, unable to bear the combined assault and the betrayal, a void was created. In that moment of utter desperation and her defiant, primal "NO," the now-uncontained Earth-Heart, thrashing in its own self-preservation, instinctively sought balance. It resonated with the only other complementary force present in the immediate vicinity that also stood in pure opposition to Kaelen's aggressive intent: the ancient, dormant lunar affinity in Sylvie's very blood.,
  • The Earth-Heart, bleeding its power, inadvertently or as a final act of re-equilibration, amplified and funneled its complementary lunar aspect directly into Sylvie. The extreme trauma and the complete magical severance acted as the violent "key," unlocking and igniting the powerful, inherited lunar magic that had lain dormant for generations. The "Sister Moon," as both a cosmic entity and a revered concept in ancient lore, answered the call, not just as a patron, but as an inherent magical force now directly connected to Sylvie's being.

In essence, Sylvie didn't just gain new magic; her ancient bloodline's dormant lunar potential was violently activated and magnified by the collapsing Earth-Heart's desperate lunge for balance, all triggered by Kaelen's destructive forces. She became a living, breathing testament to the interconnectedness of all primal forces and the unforeseen consequences of disrupting ancient harmonies.
