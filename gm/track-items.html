_Notable relics and objects the chronicle has surfaced, and where they stand. Maintained through the stylistic rewrite — append an entry (with the session it appears in) as each chronicle is rewritten._

Blessed Branch

The rekindling of a dead pyre needs one. Not a made thing — a natural rarity of the high crags, where a hardy scrub-tree now and again bears a single branch of bright blue flowers, sometimes streaked crimson, in place of white and orange. Found by chance, like a four-leaf clover, and only near the peaks. _(S1–S2)_ — Three were won in the end from Caranthalis's tree beneath the mountain: one from the Guardian's mouth, two more cut from the boughs, before goblin powder unmade the tree itself. _(S4–S5)_ — The two meant for Drosven's Gate never got there: the Askavir carried Jamal and his branches up to the peak, and Ygva tore away all three — two from Jamal, one from Sylvie — with Sylvie's heart. Every branch is now in the witch's hands. _(S6)_

The Branded Skin

The runic hide Heyou flayed from the Umbra-warped brute in the titan's tunnels — three brands cut into the flesh to compel or empower, that glowed as the dead thing dragged itself up again. He kept it to study its runes. _(S3)_

Goblin Black Powder

The goblin engineers' three-part explosive barrels, and a satchel of the same they pressed on Heyou — payment, and the promise of more, for killing the Guardian. _(S4–S5)_

Guardian's collar

A heavy collar with a broken chain, taken from the Guardian's neck — the tether that bound the beast to the Sacred Tree. _(S5)_

Seed Cones

Tight oval seed-cones gathered from the roots of Caranthalis's tree before it burned; they open only to fire. What might grow another light, should any survive. _(S5)_

Borek's Ward-Medallion

A heavy silver charm — a snarling figure ringed in metal — hung on Heyou by Borek. No key; a ward, that the Askavir might not eat him. _(S2)_

Moon Rune Jara

The rune from Sylvie's visions. Ygva's own mark. _(S2)_

The Witch's Sealed Jewelry (circlet · glassy-stone necklace · signet ring)

Locked in Ygva's rune-marked box (a moon-rune seal, her own mark) at her mountain camp: a thin circlet, a layered necklace of glassy stones, and a heavy signet ring, each with power sleeping in it. Sylvie wears the necklace; Heyou half-knew the ring's make. No one at Verrow's Span could name them — Tamber pointed to the Celsian Athenaeum. _(S7)_

Small Iron Lockbox

A keyed iron box taken from the witch's camp, still unopened; Heyou keeps it close. _(S7)_

Celsian Map

Tamber's great map: the whole of the world the party knew is a corner of it, the rest running west past knowing. _(S7)_

Pictogram Stones

Twenty-three small carved stones from the witch's camp, four laid out on a flat rock. Their order is the order of the mile-high Mountain Pictographs on the high road — a pattern Heyou recognized. _(S7)_

Askavir Heart

Cut from the fallen Askavir — a heart the size of a body, saturated with Splendor, the light once thought the gods' alone; it gives off a warmth like far sacred-pyre light. Ygva wrenched it away and Heyou pickpocketed it back and vanished with it. She wants it for calling the gods down; Sylvie is afraid to touch it. Plan: forge a fake, packed with ichor from the bird's ruined eye, to pass for the real one in the witch's hand. _(S8)_ — Pressed by an Astral Assassin below, Heyou spent the heart's power for one god-bright smite; it went cold and quiet in his hands, and he dropped unconscious. Now spent. _(S9)_

The False Heart

The forged decoy Heyou carried to hold out to Ygva in place of the real Askavir Heart — Sylvie's one condition on a plan she called foolish. _(S9)_

Stoneweaver pendant

Sylvie's stone-weaver focus from her Stoneheart Peaks craft: fixes a stone sentinel's attention, and binds and drives a great stone elemental — the Living Fortress. She handed it to Heyou to pilot the Fortress from hiding. _(S9)_
