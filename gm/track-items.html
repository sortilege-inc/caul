_Notable relics and objects the chronicle has surfaced, and where they stand. Maintained through the stylistic rewrite — append an entry (with the session it appears in) as each chronicle is rewritten._

Blessed Branch

The rekindling of a dead pyre needs one. Not a made thing — a natural rarity of the high crags, where a hardy scrub-tree now and again bears a single branch of bright blue flowers, sometimes streaked crimson, in place of white and orange. Found by chance, like a four-leaf clover, and only near the peaks. _(S1–S2)_ — Three were won in the end from Caranthalis's tree beneath the mountain: one from the Guardian's mouth, two more cut from the boughs, before goblin powder unmade the tree itself. Two now travel down to Drosven's Gate. _(S4–S5)_

The Branded Skin

The runic hide Heyou flayed from the Umbra-warped brute in the titan's tunnels — three brands cut into the flesh to compel or empower, that glowed as the dead thing dragged itself up again. He kept it to study its runes. _(S3)_

Goblin Black Powder

The goblin engineers' three-part explosive barrels, and a satchel of the same they pressed on Heyou — payment, and the promise of more, for killing the Guardian. _(S4–S5)_

Guardian's collar

A heavy collar with a broken chain, taken from the Guardian's neck — the tether that bound the beast to the Sacred Tree. _(S5)_

Seed Cones

Tight oval seed-cones gathered from the roots of Caranthalis's tree before it burned; they open only to fire. What might grow another light, should any survive. _(S5)_

Borek's Ward-Medallion

A heavy silver charm — a snarling figure ringed in metal — hung on Heyou by Borek. No key; a ward, that the Askavir might not eat him. _(S2)_

Moon Rune Jara

The rune from Sylvie's visions. Ygva's own mark. _(S2)_
