Dramatis Persona

Farah

Farah is an undead dwarf, recognized as a friend and neighbor of Jamal. She is depicted alongside other undead figures, including Ferris and Dunya.

During an encounter, Farah and the other undead dwarves react with rage towards Sylvie but hesitate upon recognizing Jamal. Farah is unsettled by Sylvie's radiant light aura, making her wary of closing in. Sylvie attempts to use Siphon Essence on the clustered undead, including Farah, but the attempt fails. Later, Sylvie successfully casts Siphon Essence, ripping the animating umbra from roughly a dozen undead dwarves, including Farah, restoring herself by their stolen essence.

Jamal is emotionally affected by the sight of Farah among the undead.
