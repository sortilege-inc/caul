Dramatis Persona

Oshel

Oshel is a witch formerly residing in Ulmira’s Step, a settlement built around the fossilized remnants of a colossal titan. She draws magic directly from the Umbra, which grants her significant power but carries a heavy stigma among the locals. The townsfolk tend to avoid her due to the strangeness associated with her magic and habits.

Oshel is the witch Sylvie had heard of on an earlier passage through the region, but she is no longer the witch of the mountain. She took Ygva as her apprentice, and it is Ygva who now keeps the Blessed Branch near the ruined mountainside shrine and watches over the community below. What became of Oshel herself is not yet recorded.

The locals' reluctance to engage with Oshel reflects a complex relationship with her, emphasizing the power dynamics at play in Ulmira’s Step.
