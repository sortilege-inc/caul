Tall Feline Katari With An Eye Patch And Broad Shoulders

The katari is tall and distinguished by an eye patch and broad shoulders. He is recognized by Heyou as a semi-independent operator with occasional ties to the Dimmers and Dimmers. His presence among a group of predominantly goblin and halfling refugees from Dolm's Break raises suspicion; Heyou believes the katari may have ulterior motives.

During the arrival of the refugees, the katari is observed remaining silent amidst the panic and chaos of the Askavir attack, which includes the evacuation of the crowd and confrontation with the attackers. His reputation and connections imply he may possess insights or intentions that are not immediately apparent, and his affiliations suggest a level of cunning and resourcefulness that may affect future interactions.
