Dramatis Persona

Savel

Savel is a giant sorcerer known for his imposing stature and mastery of the arcane arts. His gauntleted fists — with which he strikes at close range, in bursts of purple light — reflect a blend of physical strength and magical prowess. An explorer who had delved with Mellon on earlier ventures, he joined The Enduring Lesser Lights for their descent into the tunnels beneath Ulmira’s Step, where his gauntlets landed the killing blow on the Umbra-Brute. His magic is neither the Splendor of the gods nor the Umbra of the witches, but a plainer arcane sort.

In a recent venture into the caverns beneath Ulmira’s Step, Savel fought alongside Mellon, a human ranger, and Sarru, a dwarf Guardian. During their descent, he engaged a hulking goblinoid corrupted by the Umbra, delivering arcane strikes that staggered the brute as Mellon’s Great Cat companion distracted it. When the brute attempted to reanimate under the Umbra's influence, Savel's magical blows contributed to its defeat, which was ultimately achieved through the efforts of Jamal and Draz.

As the group faced ambushes from goblins and various traps, Savel remained integral to their team, using his magic to combat threats. His proactive involvement in the fray demonstrated his capabilities as both a sorcerer and a formidable ally in their dangerous journey.

Recently, Savel has shown a mechanical aspect to his character, serving as a spellcaster and support for the group. He exhibited a willingness to assist others, notably during a confrontation with a Guardian beast, where he communicated with Mellon's ghost to learn of Mellon's intent to help. Savel contributed strategically against the Guardian beast by facilitating the movement of Explosive Barrels closer to it and utilizing Mellon's ghost as a distraction. During the ensuing battle, he positioned himself alongside Jamal, Draz, Sylvie, and Sarru to provide ranged support and cast protective magic for Jamal. Hints of Savel's ties to a witch were suggested by Jamal, indicating a deeper connection to the unfolding events. He accompanied Jamal and Draz on a journey to Drosven’s Gate, navigating perilous situations and encountering supernatural forces interested in their mission.

In a subsequent encounter on the road, Savel, also known as Savel, began casting a spell that triggered unseen restraints, binding Sylvie and Heyou. He was ultimately restrained by Heyou's magic and knocked unconscious by a hidden strike. After the battle, Heyou tied Savel and Sarru's hands and stabilized them to keep them unconscious but alive.
