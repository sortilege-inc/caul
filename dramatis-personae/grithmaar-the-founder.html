Grithmaar The Founder

Grithmaar The Founder is a progenitor of Grithmaar Deep, a dwarven city known for its many levels. He is associated with the founding and governance of the city. Statues honoring him can be found throughout Grithmaar Deep, marking important locations.

Grithmaar is remembered for his pivotal role in the city's history, with the ceremonial hall serving as a place of homage where leaders are celebrated.
