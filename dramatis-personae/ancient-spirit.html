Ancient Spirit

The Ancient Spirit is a supernatural entity that has inhabited various golem bodies for approximately thirty years. It possesses a playful and curious demeanor, exploring its surroundings and the mechanical construct Heyou, which it briefly animates. The spirit communicates through subsonic rumbles, showing awareness of its environment, and provides important warnings about a powerful Guardian creature linked to a Glowing Tree, advising the party to avoid it. The Ancient Spirit claims it is not the mountain's spirit, but rather has roamed the area, displaying knowledge of local history and its own past experiences.

While exploring, the Ancient Spirit interacts with Heyou, expressing delight in its form and testing its abilities. It warns the group about the Guardian, which it identifies as the source of terrifying roars they have heard. When threatened, the spirit retreats into the Stonewraith, allowing Heyou to regain control. The presence of the Ancient Spirit adds crucial ancient knowledge and urgency to the party’s quest for Blessed Branch from the tree while emphasizing the dangers they may face.
