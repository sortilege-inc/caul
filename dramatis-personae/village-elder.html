Village Elder

The Village Elder is a notable figure in Verrow's Span, recognized as Draz's great aunt. She has a distinctive appearance with a shock of white hair resembling a wavy candle flame, along with a raised skin birthmark.

She has not seen Draz since he was an infant, having left for Verrow's Span prior to his abandonment. The elder is knowledgeable about Draz's family history, stating that his mother had died and that his father and twin siblings traveled westward toward the Amber Reach. She reveals that Draz was intentionally abandoned due to his parents' hardships, who chose to invest in his younger siblings instead.

During a gathering, the elder expresses excitement upon recognizing Draz and shares important family revelations, including her misunderstanding of his abandonment as a fosterage situation. Her insights into Draz's family dynamics are pivotal in shaping his character and motivations.
