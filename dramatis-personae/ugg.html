Ugg

Ugg and his brother Grug are giants who served as muscle for a syndicate — a rival to the crew Heyou ran with (which was based out of the cliff-carved port of Salthewn). The brothers are near-identical save for their height: one stands about nine feet, the other closer to ten and a half. Between the two of them they have four eyes — Ugg has but a single eye, and with it a poor sense of depth.

Heyou knew them from his syndicate days, chiefly across a card table. Ugg's lone eye and bad depth perception made him easy to cheat, and Heyou fleeced him regularly — the trick was that the brothers wore glasses that made them hard to tell apart, so a swindler had to be sure which giant he was sitting down against before dealing.

Status: Recalled fondly, from a safe distance.
