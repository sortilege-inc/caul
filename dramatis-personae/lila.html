Dramatis Persona

Lila

Lila is a great-aunt to Jamal and the grandmother of Bufa, an explorer knowledgeable about ruins and holdout communities. She has a nurturing connection with Jamal, who shares her interest in exploration and wilderness stories.

Bufa and Jamal bond over their shared enthusiasm for adventure and the history of the mountains, suggesting a close family dynamic that enhances Jamal's character. This relationship offers Jamal guidance and potential resources for adventures focused on the west side of the mountains, known for its rich history of ruins and dangerous creatures. Bufa's experiences and knowledge may significantly aid Jamal in his ongoing journey.
