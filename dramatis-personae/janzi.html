Dramatis Persona

Janzi

Janzi is a simiah individual from the mountain settlement of Verrow's Span, known for its carved homes and rope bridges. They exhibit unique physiology adapted for climbing and navigating difficult terrains and embody a sense of community and hospitality.

Recently, Janzi greeted Draz as “cousin,” reflecting familial connections within the simiah community. They offered food, drink, and rest to travelers, adhering to the customs of hospitality. During a tour of Verrow's Span, Janzi showcased the settlement's significance as a connection point to other regions, suggesting a role as a bridge between their community and newcomers.

In addition to their welcoming nature, Janzi is responsible for overseeing the preservation of the Askavir body for examination by the party. The body has been kept unmolested, reflecting a careful approach to its handling. Janzi confirmed they ensured the body was preserved and coordinated the effort to move it, with a wheeled platform being constructed for this purpose. They expressed a willingness to assist in processing valuable parts from the Askavir for the party while leaving the remainder for the community as payment for handling the remains. Janzi suggested that the party could take their time with the examination, indicating a collaborative approach to the task.

Status: Welcoming host and community connector, diligent caretaker of the Askavir remains.
