Inola Wending, Keeper of Splendor

Inola is a middle-aged Simia known for her radiant white-hot Splendor and her role as a source of magical energy. She has a warm demeanor, welcoming others with open arms and referring to Sylvie as "dazzling." Inola occasionally retreats to the Barn for solitude, seeking respite from the busyness of the dean and students.

During a dinner with Sylvie, Inola discusses her role in the compound and the concept of Ascension, identifying Sylvie and Heyou as potential candidates. She introduces Casper tol-Otheidas, the great-grandson of the God-King Otheidas, and explains that only The Elect may ascend, emphasizing the necessity of self-salvation for those chosen. Inola's interactions indicate her influence and authority within the compound's magical and political dynamics.
