Mollthain's Warden

Beast — the pet or avatar of Mollthain, the Rampart

Mollthain's Warden is the ancient monster that lurks in the depths beneath Grithmaar Deep, identified as the pet or avatar of the departed god Mollthain, the Rampart. It is the "great creature left by Mollthain" that Ygva directed the party to seek — a being that either holds or embodies Mollthain's essence, whose power she hopes to harvest for the god-pillars of her great pyre. It is the same monster Jamal's people once broke into and, out of sheer terror, dug away from rather than face; the party believes it tied to the ruin of Grithmaar Deep and Jamal's home.

The Warden was first glimpsed through Sylvie's Umbral Raven, sent to scout the corridors below Grithmaar Deep amid assembled enemies. The Warden detected the raven and tore its essence apart, annihilating it. Jamal recognized the described being as the ancient monster the party had come to confront.

Sylvie's unseen dream-patron offered methods for obtaining the Warden's power: destroy it and take its remains, rebuild or transform it into a vessel, or siphon its essence into a suitable container. To that end the party plans to attempt channeling captured Splendor into the Living Fortress. As of the assault on Grithmaar Deep, the party held a fortified position with the Warden still ahead of them, deeper in the dark.

Not to be confused with the Guardian — the purple, multi-eyed feline demon that guarded the Sacred Tree and was destroyed in the Woodland — an entirely separate creature.
