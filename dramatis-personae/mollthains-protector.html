Mollthain's Protector

Guardian, Colossal Guardian

A colossal guardian resembling a five-story structure, radiating overwhelming Splendor energy. It was placed there by Mollthain to steward a local light. The guardian is impervious to fireball attacks and can raise crystalline walls, creating barriers in combat. During an encounter, it wept molten streams and formed protective barriers, igniting under subsequent attacks. It rejected requests to stand down, shaking the chamber.
