God-King Otheidas (2)

Otheidas

God-King Otheidas is associated with a shrine featuring motifs of crowns and scepters. His great-grandson, Casper tol-Otheidas, indicates a lineage connected to him. Otheidas attempted to ascend with the entire population of a realm but ultimately succeeded only in ascending alone, leading to chaos.

The Keeper Of Splendor, Inola, expresses reverence for Otheidas's legacy despite the complexities of his rule and disappearance. Draz identifies a shrine with motifs matching those of Otheidas. It is noted that while Otheidas was not a cruel king, the role of kingship itself carries intrinsic cruelty. Otheidas's actions and their consequences have significantly impacted the current circumstances faced by the characters.
