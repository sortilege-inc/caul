Dramatis Persona

Ulmira

Ulmira is the goddess of pacts within the Splendor Domain, emphasizing trust and collaboration among beings. She is associated with other deities including Tlellic, Kreth, Brathis, Mollthain, Caranthalis, Ossa, and God-King Otheidas.

Ygva, a powerful witch, seeks remnants and totems of the gods, including Ulmira, to draw them into the World Below with the aid of titans and prevent the upper world's ruin. She plans to amass god-touched artifacts, specifically mentioning her intention to present fuller offerings to Ulmira due to a significant interest in her artifacts. Ygva believes that the involvement of Ulmira is vital to restoring the balance of power in the world.

Ygva's ultimate objective is to create a great fire to lure the gods back down, claiming this action is crucial for the restoration of life in the world above.
