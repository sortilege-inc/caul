Dramatis Persona

You Bastard

Kalisk companion

Kalisk, known informally as You Bastard, was a massive horned beast resembling a moose, standing approximately double the size of a horse, with a bony structure around its facial and shoulder areas. Its flat nose and prominent bony ridges gave it a distinctive appearance, while its hindquarters featured terraced fungus growths. Kalisk possessed a robust physique, enabling it to endure significant stress and damage, and it exhibited a strong ability to graze on thorny, fibrous weeds, favoring challenging plants over softer grasses. Initially collared by Jamal, a dwarf warrior and member of the party, Kalisk was successfully led back to camp, though its unruly demeanor became evident, particularly under stress. Despite this, it showcased intelligence by responding to commands from Jamal and his allies.

Jamal observed Kalisk grazing on dense foliage outside the camp, which allowed him to mount the creature. Utilizing a satchel filled with crushed thistles, brambles, and flowers, he roused Kalisk, who accepted him before proceeding down the road. During an ambush, Jamal rode Kalisk into the fray, attempting a trampling strike against their foes. As the battle escalated, Kalisk became increasingly stressed, complicating Jamal's control over it. In a fierce encounter with a dragon, Kalisk was grappled by the creature yet managed to avoid trampling allies while repositioning for an attack, demonstrating restraint and awareness of its surroundings.

Recognizing Kalisk's potential as a means of escape and for trampling through obstacles, particularly against groups of mutated townsfolk, Jamal's bond with the creature deepened. Following a battle, Draz attempted to calm Kalisk with soothing words, resulting in a headbutt but no further aggression. Using Nature's Tongue, Draz communicated with Kalisk and discovered that its kin referred to it by the same name, "Kalisk," and learned of its preference for big spiny thistles. Kalisk responded positively to foraging for these thistles, indicating a budding level of cooperation with the party, while its formidable presence offered a strategic advantage as they navigated the dangers of Ulmira's Step.

More recently, during the exploration of Grithmaar Deep, Kalisk charged forward to intercept an attack from a horned, armored figure, bravely sustaining a spear hit in the process. In a subsequent encounter, it repelled a goblinoid counterattack alongside Jamal, enduring poison damage while successfully eliminating multiple attackers. In combat, Kalisk demonstrated its tactical capabilities by effectively engaging an Astral Assassin, successfully striking it from behind. When a guardian creature expressed displeasure at the party's request to stand down, Kalisk remained focused on the threats, prepared to defend against incoming attacks. Kalisk continued to demonstrate resilience, unwavering loyalty, and a willingness to engage directly with enemies, acting as a protective force for the party.

Tragically, the Kalisk companion was later found drowned in Grithmaar Deep, associated with a collar recovered after its demise. Heyou returned underwater to retrieve the Blessed Branch and the Kalisk’s collar. The Kalisk's death was noted, and it was suggested that it may not have been alive to begin with. The collar was removed from the drowned Kalisk, and the party discussed the possibility of finding a suitable replacement companion.
