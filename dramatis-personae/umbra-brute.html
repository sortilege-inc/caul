Umbra-Brute

Hulking Brute, Brute, Ogre

Umbra-Brute is a hulking goblinoid creature, nearly ten feet tall, characterized by immense bulk and shadow-warped sinew. Its distorted features include eyes that burn with the corruption of the Umbra. The brute wields a massive cudgel, made of solid wood and stone bindings, capable of delivering devastating blows. Its presence radiates a menacing aura, connected to the dark forces of the Umbra.

In a cavern encounter, the Umbra-Brute charged at the party and struck Jamal with a powerful blow. During the chaos, Heyou attacked from the creature's blind side, wounding it and causing ichor to burst forth. This ichor ignited upon contact with the slicks on the walls, setting the area ablaze. The fight continued as Jamal and others coordinated their attacks. Savel’s arcane gauntlets landed the finishing blow, and the brute collapsed — but with its dying action it hurled a boulder that battered Draz, Sarru, and Sylvie. It did not stay down: some while later it dragged itself back up, glowing with Umbra light, and swung blindly (one eye ruined) before Draz put an arrow through its skull a second time and Jamal and Heyou destroyed its head outright to keep it from rising again.

The key to its rising lay on its chest: among older cut scars were three symbols of a different make — not carved but branded, and they glowed just before the creature reanimated. Sylvie recognized the power in them as the same Umbra-sort the community fears — a power that opposes the sacred pyres and stands at enmity with her kind — meant to compel or empower whatever bears the mark. Heyou cut the branded patch of skin away and kept it to study (see The Branded Skin).
