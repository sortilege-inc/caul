Umbra-Brute

Hulking Brute, Brute, Ogre

Umbra-Brute is a hulking goblinoid creature, nearly ten feet tall, characterized by immense bulk and shadow-warped sinew. Its distorted features include eyes that burn with the corruption of the Umbra. The brute wields a massive cudgel, made of solid wood and stone bindings, capable of delivering devastating blows. Its presence radiates a menacing aura, connected to the dark forces of the Umbra.

In a cavern encounter, the Umbra-Brute charged at the party and struck Jamal with a powerful blow. During the chaos, Heyou attacked from the creature's blind side, wounding it and causing ichor to burst forth. This ichor ignited upon contact with the slicks on the walls, setting the area ablaze. The fight continued as Jamal and others coordinated their attacks. The Umbra-Brute was ultimately defeated by a combined strike from Jamal's warhammer and Draz's arrow, which caved in its skull, ending its threat.
