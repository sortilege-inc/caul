Dramatis Persona

Mollthain

Mollthain, known as the god of the rampart, stands among the eight deities of the divine pantheon, embodying strength and protection. He is closely associated with supernatural forces, having served alongside other gods such as Caranthalis and Tlellic. Mollthain is intricately linked to Ygva's plans to gather god-touched artifacts and totems aimed at invoking the gods into the World Below.

Beneath Grithmaar Deep lies a creature believed to embody Mollthain's essence or guard something significant connected to him. This guardian, formed by a greater being, is tasked with protecting a profusion of life that thrives on an alternative energy source, akin to a coral reef, and was left behind by Mollthain to steward a small local light source after a dominant outside light departed. This connection indicates that Mollthain plays a crucial role in the balance of energy within the cave ecosystem. The creature is intertwined with the destruction of Jamal's home, casting a long shadow over the dwarf tunnel-runner and instilling fear among the dwarves, who now avoid exploring the area further. The offerings made to Mollthain are meager in comparison to those for the other gods, highlighting the urgency for balancing the artifacts before a significant summoning can take place, thus establishing Mollthain's role within the larger narrative of divine intervention and the struggle for power.

Recently, the party confirmed their mission to seek the creature connected to Mollthain, having engaged in discussions with Ygva, who accepted their intention. They recognized the necessity of acquiring a Blessed Branch, a sacred item required to rekindle a pyre at Drosven’s Gate, critical to their objectives. Ygva agreed to provide a branch from the Caranthalis Altar, emphasizing the importance of replacing it should her cultivation efforts for the offered seeds fail.

The group traversed Verrow's Span, where they encountered refugees and secured the body of Askavir for examination. Sylvie's dreams and the conversations among the party reflect their ties to the Old Gods, including Mollthain. Amidst a severe blizzard, Sylvie demonstrated her magical prowess by conjuring a Wall of Flame, showcasing the harsh conditions they faced. The party's determination to return to Grithmaar Deep underscores the ongoing significance of Mollthain's legacy in their quest to recover the Living Fortress and deliver the Blessed Branch as a vital element of their journey.

In recent developments, Mollthain has revealed itself as an ancient monster that resides below Grithmaar Deep, renowned for its immense power and connection to Splendor magic. The party aims to harness this power for the god pillars, exploring various methods such as destruction, reconstruction, or siphoning its essence into a suitable vessel. Jamal identified Mollthain as their target, while Sylvie sent her Umbral Raven to scout, only for it to be destroyed by Mollthain, confirming its ominous presence. Following this, Sylvie's patron provided guidance on harvesting power from Mollthain, stressing the importance of capturing its essence without destruction. The party is fortifying their position near Grithmaar Deep, preparing for their next move against the Guardian.
