Ossa

Ossa, The Wanderer of the Peaks, Ossa, the Wanderer of the Peaks

Ossa is one of the eight deities in the divine pantheon, known as the Wanderer of the Peaks. She is associated with significant supernatural forces, particularly tied to the sky and mountainous regions, and linked to the Splendor Domain. Ossa possesses the ability to traverse mountain peaks, reflecting her affinity for heights and a deep connection to the elements of the sky.

Recently, Draz discovered that a compass he owns points toward Ossa, suggesting she may have left behind an artifact or essence in the area. This revelation emerged during a discussion about collecting totems and remnants of the gods, as Ygva aims to draw the gods back to the World Below. Ossa's significance to their quest has marked her location as a target for exploration. The party is considering the challenges in accessing the skyward path associated with her, while Draz expresses curiosity about her nature and contemplates whether the direction indicated by the compass could lead them to her or provide further insight into her importance in their current endeavors.
