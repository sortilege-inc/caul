Dramatis Persona

Grug

Grug and his brother Ugg are giants who worked as muscle for a syndicate rival to Heyou's old crew. The brothers are near-identical save for height — one stands about nine feet, the other closer to ten and a half — and between them they have four eyes: where Ugg has a single eye and a poor sense of depth, Grug has three and misses nothing.

Heyou remembers Grug as the one he could never cheat. The brothers wore glasses that made them hard to tell apart, and more than one of Heyou's syndicate friends was knocked flat for trying to swindle Grug at a card game.

Status: Sharp-eyed and not to be swindled.
