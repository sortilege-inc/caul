Dramatis Persona

Bufa

Bufa, grandson of Lila, is a west-ranging explorer with a strong connection to the wilderness. He is dedicated to exploring ruins and holdout communities along the western side of the mountains, an area known for its remnants of civilization and dangerous monsters. Bufa seeks to engage with nature and uncover the hidden aspects of the land.

Bufa has formed a bond through shared stories of exploration, particularly showing interest in the abandoned town of Larrow and coastal regions. He can provide valuable information and guidance for those who decide to journey west.
