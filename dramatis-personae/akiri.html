Dramatis Persona

Akiri

Akiri, a simiah, resembles an anthropomorphic monkey with a primarily white coat and dark brown streaks. He is part of Verrow's Span, a community in the mountains inhabited mostly by simiah, alongside some human and Katari families.

Akiri welcomes travelers with hospitality, offering food, drink, and rest while guiding newcomers through the village. He has knowledge of the local area and connections to important routes, including Tern’s Watch and St. Thera’s Remit, a nearby monastery.

When Draz arrives at Verrow's Span, Akiri and local Janzi greet him as a cousin and inquire about the needs of the travelers. He expresses a willingness to show them the village and engages in local customs of sharing meals and stories, reflecting the social nature of the simiah. Akiri helps facilitate connections within the community, assisting newcomers in acclimating to their surroundings and exploring the region.
