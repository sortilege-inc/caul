Yuki

Mellon’s Great Cat

[[Mellon’s Great Cat]], a large feline companion known for its formidable presence and fierce loyalty, has a thick, powerful build suited for rugged environments such as caverns and mountains. This great cat typically stays close to its master, Mellon, displaying an alert demeanor and readiness for combat. During an expedition into the caverns, the great cat exhibited agility and strength by lunging at a hulking brute, raking its legs and providing crucial support to the party. Amid chaos as flames ignited, it distracted the enemy, allowing its companions to strike. When Mellon broke ranks and sprinted into a crevice, the great cat remained behind, distressed as it yowled and scraped at the rubble sealing its companion's path. This reaction demonstrated its emotional bond with Mellon, pacing anxiously with a lashing tail and raised hackles, revealing its concern for its missing ally.

Presently, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]], affectionately known as [[Mellon’s Great Cat]], accompanies the party during their adventures in the Mountain Pass. Upon Melon's disappearance in an avalanche while attempting to rescue [[Mellon’s Great Cat]], the feline's role evolved. During the preparation for an ambush, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] was strategically positioned alongside Jamal, Sylvie, and Sarru Savel as visible bait on the lower path, showcasing its involvement in their plans despite being a non-combatant. While waiting for the ambush to unfold, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] engaged in games and conversation with the party, contributing to their morale during a tense period. Following the ambush, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] chose to follow Draz along the upper path, indicating a bond or instinctual connection to him following Melon's disappearance.

In recent developments, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] is often seen alongside Mellon, who has now become a ghost at the party's camp. Despite Mellon’s incorporeal nature, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] tolerates his presence and appears comfortable around him. When Mellon interacts with the cat, his attempts to pet her do not affect her fur, signifying their supernatural bond. During Mellon’s communications with the party, the cat sits with him in a seemingly normal routine despite Mellon’s underlying melancholy. While the party engages in their mission, [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] remains a silent companion, primarily serving as a presence rather than an active participant. In the aftermath of a catastrophic explosion that destroys a Sacred Tree, both [[Mellon’s Great Cat]] and Mellon exhibit more spectral characteristics, observing the consequences without physical ties to the living world.
