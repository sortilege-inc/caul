Dramatis Persona

Dagga Brunnvar

Dagga

Dagga Brunnvar is an antiquarian dwarf known for her distinct mustache. She welcomed the party at the Celsian Compound and informed them that visitors who teleport should contribute appropriate artifacts to the treasury. Dagga examined a wooden left leg presented by Heyou and noted that he had “a glow,” indicating that the Keeper Of Splendor would likely wish to see him.

Dagga escorted the party through the compound, explaining the accommodations and layout of the student barracks. She expressed concern for the safety of the party's crab, Pinchy, and requested that it be left outside. When Sylvie inquired about a sealed door resonating with her magic, Dagga refused to disclose its contents, stating it was the dean's purview. Later, Dagga asked Jamal for his loyalty and expressed interest in seeing him again. During a tense encounter with Dean Therion, Dagga prepared to protect Jamal rather than engage in direct conflict.
