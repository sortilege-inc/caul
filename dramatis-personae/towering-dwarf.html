Dramatis Persona

Towering Dwarf

axe-wielding foe, fleeing axe-wielder, wielder, towering 'dwarf' (a human)

Moniker and reputation: The Towering Dwarf, known for his significant height among humans.

Motives or pressure points: He wields the rumored Axe Of Great Value, which he struggles to carry due to its size. His retreat indicates a desire to escape despite injury.

Evidence:

  • Engaged by Draz, who shot an arrow at his wrist, impairing his ability to wield the axe.
  • Continued to drag the heavy axe away in retreat despite sustaining damage.
  • Shot again in the knee by Draz, slowing his escape.
  • Tackled by Jamal, resulting in his shattering into brittle bones.
  • Left the Axe Of Great Value behind for the party to recover.

Angles: Vulnerable due to injury, slow escape, reliance on the axe for combat.

Status: Defeated.
