Tariq The Mason

Tariq The Mason is a legendary forebear of Jamal, renowned for his craftsmanship in masonry. He is often depicted holding a mason's trowel. Tariq is celebrated among heroic figures in the dwarven city of Grithmaar Deep, where his statue stands.

Tariq was known for slaying numerous orcs before his demise.
