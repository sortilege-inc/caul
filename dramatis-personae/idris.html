Dramatis Persona

Idris

Idris is a celebrated dwarven warrior, recognized for his formidable presence and combat prowess. He is depicted in a statue within Grithmaar Deep.

Idris emerged among a cluster of undead dwarves in a flooded ceremonial hall, initially hesitating as he approached Sylvie and Jamal. He attempted to attack but was obstructed by the throng of undead bodies. When Sylvie unleashed a Power Push, Idris was among those hurled back, clearing space around the party. The floodwaters surged, forcing the group to react quickly as Idris and the other undead pressed forward.
