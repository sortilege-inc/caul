Dramatis Persona

Tlellic

Tlellic is the dark god of predation and the long night, embodying hunger and darkness while associated with shadows and malevolence. His symbols are marked in the caverns alongside those of Kreth, indicating his territorial influence and enhancing the sinister atmosphere of the explored crevice. He is one of the eight gods of the pantheon.

Recently, Tlellic has become a significant entity in the region's lore, as discovered by the character Heyou. This indicates a connection to ancient practices and historical sites, revealing a role in the spiritual and magical aspects of the world. The oppressive atmosphere surrounding his domain featured an oily slick, later recognized as a highly flammable secretion from an ooze, which linked it directly to Tlellic's influence. Illustrations of shrines dedicated to Tlellic discovered in ancient texts by Heyou further emphasize this link, suggesting his presence is intertwined with the remnants of a past civilization and its worship practices.

The chaotic encounter with a hulking brute, corrupted by the Umbra, further underscored Tlellic’s influence, as its predatory nature reflected the dark god's essence. The adventurers’ awareness of Tlellic’s presence significantly impacted their strategies as they navigated the treacherous landscape. Tlellic communicates through intermediaries, notably Kelun, who serves as a mouthpiece for multiple deities, including Tlellic. Kelun’s address to the party revealed his role in representing Tlellic, Caranthalis, Ossa, and Mollthain, highlighting Tlellic’s relevance in the narrative surrounding the Sacred Tree and the implications of its destruction.

The Askavir, a giant bird believed to be Tlellic's mount, familiar, or avatar, demonstrates his predatory nature and the chaos it incites. Sylvie studied the runes and texts related to Tlellic, discovering the connection between the rune and the god. As the Askavir attacked refugees, its actions reflected Tlellic's instincts, leading to chaos and destruction. Its demise revealed an aura of Splendor magic, indicating Tlellic's divine potency and remnants of his essence within the Askavir's heart and eyes. Sylvie's attempt to siphon this power highlighted the immense nature of Tlellic's magic. The aftermath of the Askavir's death prompted discussions about the significance of its bones, suggesting Tlellic's continued influence even after the creature's demise.

Tlellic is also central to the gathering of god-touched artifacts and offerings, particularly in connection to the construction of a pyre for the gods. His altar is one of eight, each dedicated to a different god, intended to draw the gods into the inner world and bind them with the aid of titans. The heart of the Askavir is placed on Tlellic's designated stone during ritual preparations. The character Ygva plans to gather remnants and totems of the gods, including Tlellic, in an effort to prevent the ruin of the upper world. This involves awakening the gods and utilizing the power of the pyre, emphasizing a strategy that balances offerings among the altars.

Recently, Tlellic has been associated with the departed god of the same name in the context of the creature known as Askavir. The remains of Askavir, previously encountered by the party, are noted to be abandoned, pending relocation. The group has established a targeted harvest list for valuable parts from Askavir's body, particularly the claws, beak, and eyes. Sylvie has expressed interest in taking Askavir's eyes to mount as a natural deterrent on her bag. The body is preserved for examination, and preparations are made to move it. The party collaborates on the harvesting process, sharing knowledge and resources to benefit their community through their findings.
