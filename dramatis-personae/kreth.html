Dramatis Persona

Kreth

Kreth, the dark god of deception and the moon, is depicted in reliefs along stairways in caverns. His symbols, found alongside those of Tlellic, the dark god of predation, indicate a shared dominion over shadowy forces. His iconography suggests an underlying influence in the treacherous environments encountered by delvers. Kreth is also associated with false visions and deceit.

As the delvers descended into the caverns, they experienced an oppressive atmosphere characterized by a kerosene-like odor and stone walls marked by dark, oily slicks. During their descent, they confronted a hulking goblinoid creature corrupted by the Umbra, which was ultimately defeated through coordinated efforts among the delvers. The creature's ichor ignited, causing a chain reaction that set the cavern ablaze, revealing the volatile nature of the surroundings.

The delvers executed tactical maneuvers to navigate traps and ambushes while battling goblins fortifying their positions. The pervasive danger and corruption throughout the caverns underscored Kreth's influence as the group decided to press onward, ready to confront the goblin forces in their lair.

Ygva intends to gather god-touched artifacts, including those linked to Kreth, as part of a plan to draw the gods back into the inner world and bind them with titanic aid. Discussions regarding artifacts and totems tied to various gods mention a location called Faerie's Guard in Thistleglass as a potential site to seek something related to Kreth. The need to find remnants or totems of the gods, including Kreth, is essential to assist in larger goals. Kreth's significance is crucial in their quest to restore balance and power to their world.
