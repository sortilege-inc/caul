Dramatis Persona

Stonewraith

Stone Golem, Stone Creature

Stonewraith is a ten-foot-tall creature that emerges from the stone walls of the Underground Complex and possesses resistance to conventional weaponry. Its attacks produce deep subsonic rumbles that disrupt the environment, capable of knocking opponents off their perches and dealing damage. It can retaliate against ranged attacks, showcasing its formidable nature.

During recent encounters, Draz attempted to attack the Stonewraith, only for his arrow to be pushed back, indicating the creature's unusual defenses. The Stonewraith's subsonic attack caused falling rocks that damaged Draz but did not penetrate his armor. Sylvie approached the Stonewraith with open hands, and the creature responded attentively, suggesting a form of communication that transcends typical understanding.

Heyou engaged with the Stonewraith, recognizing it as an ancient entity linked to a magical relay system. This interaction allowed the spirit within the Stonewraith to temporarily inhabit Heyou's body, revealing that it had roamed the area for thirty years and provided warnings about the Guardian of the Glowing Tree, a powerful entity. The Stonewraith's actions and responses reflect its connection to ancient technology and supernatural elements surrounding the party's quest.
